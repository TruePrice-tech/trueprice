Architectural Shingle Roof Cost (2026)

Architectural shingles are the most common roofing choice for homeowners because they usually balance cost, appearance, and lifespan better than basic 3 tab shingles.

In many markets, architectural shingle roof replacement costs often fall in the range of about $5.50 to $9.00 per square foot installed, though actual quotes vary with roof size, tear off requirements, complexity, ventilation work, flashing scope, and local labor rates.

For many homeowners, total project pricing lands somewhere between roughly $10,000 and $24,000, with larger or more complex roofs pricing above that range.

What affects architectural shingle roof pricing

  • Roof size and pitch
  • Number of valleys, dormers, skylights, and penetrations
  • Tear off and disposal requirements
  • Underlayment and flashing details
  • Ventilation upgrades
  • Warranty coverage
  • Regional labor and permit costs

Architectural shingles vs 3 tab shingles

Architectural shingles generally cost more than 3 tab shingles, but many homeowners choose them because they usually offer a thicker profile, better curb appeal, and longer expected lifespan.

That does not automatically make every quote fair. Some contractors charge premium pricing without clearly upgrading the full roofing system around the shingles.
