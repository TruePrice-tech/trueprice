Roofing in Arlington Heights: what locals should know

Weather & climate

Arlington Heights in Chicagoland sees moderate hail from spring and summer storms, with harsh winters bringing snow, ice dams, and freeze-thaw cycling that stress roofs

🧱

Best materials for Arlington Heights

Architectural shingles rated for cold climates with strong freeze-thaw resistance are recommended in Arlington Heights, with ice and water shield at all eaves and valleys

📅

Local market

Arlington Heights contractors are busiest during spring and summer; scheduling your roof replacement in fall or early winter can yield better pricing

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new roof cost in Arlington Heights?

Most homeowners in Arlington Heights pay between $6,650 and $65,800 for a complete roof replacement depending on roof size and materials.

Why do roofing quotes vary so much?

Roofing estimates vary depending on labor assumptions, material brands, flashing work, ventilation upgrades, and warranty coverage included in the proposal.

Should I always choose the lowest roofing quote?

Not always. Lower bids sometimes exclude items like flashing, ventilation upgrades, or disposal costs that appear later as change orders.

My home in Arlington Heights is older. Does that affect the cost?

Often yes. Homes in Arlington Heights average around 41 years old. Older roofs may need additional decking repair, updated ventilation, or code-required upgrades that add to the base replacement cost.
