Roof Replacement Cost in Atlanta GA

The average roof replacement cost in Atlanta GA typically ranges from $9,500 to $18,500 depending on roof size, roofing material, tear off requirements, pitch, and overall complexity.

For many homeowners, a standard architectural shingle roof replacement in Atlanta falls in the $11,500 to $16,500 range, though premium materials and complex rooflines can increase total cost.

Typical Roofing Prices in Atlanta GA

  • Architectural shingles: $450 to $700 per square
  • Metal roofing: $900 to $1,500 per square
  • Tile roofing: $1,300 to $2,800 per square

What Affects Roof Replacement Cost in Atlanta GA?

  • Roof size and number of stories
  • Roof pitch and jobsite complexity
  • Material selection
  • Local labor costs and permit requirements
  • Tear off and disposal needs
  • Flashing, ventilation, and decking repairs

How to Compare Roofing Quotes in Atlanta GA

Do not compare roofing quotes by price alone. A lower quote may exclude flashing replacement, ventilation upgrades, underlayment quality, tear off, disposal, or warranty coverage.

The best way to compare quotes is to review total scope, roofing materials, workmanship warranty, manufacturer warranty, cleanup, and clearly stated exclusions.
