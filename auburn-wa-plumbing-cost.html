Plumbing in Auburn: what locals should know

💧

Water quality

The soft water in Auburn is a plus for plumbing longevity. Water heaters last longer and pipes stay cleaner. Focus your plumbing budget on other priorities like pipe material and drainage.

❄

Freeze risk

Auburn occasionally sees freezing temperatures that can threaten exposed pipes. Basic insulation on outdoor spigots and crawlspace pipes is a smart, inexpensive precaution.

🔧

Material recommendation

Most Auburn plumbers now recommend PEX for whole-house repiping. It's code-compliant, durable, and significantly cheaper than copper. The main exception: some homeowners prefer copper for its longevity and perceived quality.

💡

Local tip

Homes built in Auburn during the 1970s-80s often have copper pipes that may be developing pinhole leaks after 40+ years. If you're seeing green staining at joints, it's time for an inspection.
