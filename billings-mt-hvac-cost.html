HVAC in Billings: what locals should know

🌡

Climate and your HVAC

Billings sees some of the coldest temperatures in the lower 48. Dual-fuel systems (heat pump + gas furnace backup) are gaining popularity for their efficiency in moderate cold while maintaining gas reliability for extreme days.

⚙

System recommendation

In Billings's extreme cold, a gas furnace rated 96%+ AFUE is the safest choice. Standard heat pumps lose efficiency below 20°F, though cold-climate heat pumps now work down to -15°F.

📅

Best time to buy

In Billings, replace your furnace in late summer before the cold sets in. A mid-winter furnace failure is an emergency — you'll pay rush pricing and have fewer options.

💡

Local tip

If you're in Billings, make sure your new furnace installation includes proper combustion air supply and venting. High-efficiency condensing furnaces require specific venting that differs from older units.
