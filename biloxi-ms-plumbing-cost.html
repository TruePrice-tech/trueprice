Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a plumber cost in Biloxi?

Plumber rates in Biloxi typically range from $85-$150/hour. Total project costs depend on the scope — a simple drain cleaning may cost $200-$450 while a water heater replacement runs $1,500-$4,500.

Should I repair or replace my water heater?

If your water heater is over 10 years old or the repair cost exceeds 50% of replacement, replacing is usually the better investment. Tankless water heaters last 20+ years and can reduce energy costs.

Is PEX or copper better for repiping?

PEX is 30-40% cheaper than copper and faster to install. Copper lasts longer (50+ years vs 25-30 for PEX) and has higher resale value. Both are code-compliant in most areas.

Home stock in Biloxi. Biloxi's housing stock averages 34 years old — relatively recent construction that was largely built to modern code. plumbing work projects here tend to be simpler: fewer surprise compliance issues, quicker permit turnaround, and contractors who can quote from memory.
