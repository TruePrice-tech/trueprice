Common concrete questions in Bowie

What is the best time to pour concrete?

Pour when temperatures stay between 50-80F for at least 3 days. Spring and fall are ideal. Avoid pouring in rain, direct hot sun, or below freezing. Cold and heat both compromise cure quality.

How thick should a concrete driveway be?

Residential driveways should be 4 inches thick for cars and 5-6 inches for heavy vehicles like RVs. A 6-inch compacted gravel base underneath is essential. Thickened edges (6 inches) prevent cracking at the perimeter.

Is concrete or asphalt better for a driveway?

Concrete lasts 30+ years vs. 15-20 for asphalt. Concrete costs more upfront ($6-$12/sqft vs. $3-$6/sqft) but needs less maintenance. Asphalt needs resealing every 3-5 years and is softer in extreme heat.

Do I need a permit for concrete work in Bowie?

In Bowie, permits are typically required for driveways, sidewalks, and any concrete work near property lines or public right-of-way. Backyard patios may be exempt depending on size. Check local requirements.

More questions about concrete in Bowie

What does a concrete driveway cost per square foot?

Plain 4-inch concrete runs $6-$12 per square foot installed in 2026. Stamped concrete adds $4-$8/sqft for pattern and color. Heavy-duty 6-inch driveways for RVs or trucks cost $9-$15/sqft. A 600 sq ft two-car driveway typically lands between $5,800 and $8,100.

How much does a 1,000 sq ft concrete patio cost?

Typical 2026 ranges for a 1,000 sq ft project: plain broom-finish $8,000-$12,000, exposed-aggregate $9,500-$14,500, stamped $11,000-$16,500. Tearing out an existing slab adds $1,500-$3,000. Reinforcing wire mesh and rebar are usually $0.50-$1.00/sqft and worth it on driveways, optional on patios.

What does a slab foundation cost in Bowie?

A monolithic slab foundation for a typical single-family house in Bowie runs $5,000-$15,000 depending on square footage and soil conditions. A garage slab is $2,500-$6,000. Full basement foundations are a different scope entirely and run $15,000-$30,000.

Do I need a permit for concrete work in Bowie?

Driveways, patios over a certain size, and any structural slab in Bowie require a permit and an inspection of the base prep and rebar before the pour. Sidewalks, repairs, and resurfacing usually do not. Your contractor pulls the permit — never pull it in your own name.
