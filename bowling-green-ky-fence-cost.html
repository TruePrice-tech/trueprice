Frequently asked fencing questions

Does a fence increase property value?

A well-built fence adds 1-5% to property value depending on material and neighborhood norms. Privacy fencing adds more value in neighborhoods where it is expected. Chain link adds minimal value.

How long does a wood fence last?

Pressure-treated pine lasts 15-20 years. Cedar lasts 15-25 years. Redwood lasts 20-30 years. All wood fences last longer with stain or sealant applied every 2-3 years.

How tall can I build my fence?

Most municipalities allow 6 feet in backyards and 3-4 feet in front yards without a variance. Some HOAs restrict fence height further. Always check local zoning before building.

What is the cheapest fence material?

Chain link is the cheapest at $10-$20 per linear foot installed. Pressure-treated pine is the cheapest wood option at $15-$25/ft. Vinyl and composite start at $20-$30/ft but never need painting.

How long does a wood fence last in Bowling Green?

In Bowling Green's climate, a properly installed and maintained pressure-treated fence lasts 15-20 years. Cedar lasts 15-25 years. Untreated pine may only last 5-8 years. Staining or sealing every 2-3 years and keeping vegetation trimmed away from the base are the two most effective ways to extend your fence's lifespan.

Do I need a permit to build a fence in Bowling Green?

Fence permits are required in most Bowling Green jurisdictions, particularly for heights over 6 feet. Before breaking ground: call 811 for utility locates, verify property lines (a survey costs $300-$600 if boundaries are unclear), and check HOA requirements. A professional contractor handles the permit process as part of the project.

How much does fence installation cost per foot in Bowling Green?

Per-foot costs for fence installation in Bowling Green: wood privacy $15-$30, vinyl $20-$40, chain link $10-$20, and aluminum or wrought iron $25-$55. A standard 150-foot backyard fence runs $2,250-$6,000 total. Always compare 3 quotes with the same scope and materials to ensure you are getting fair pricing.
