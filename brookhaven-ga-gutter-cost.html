Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a gutter cost in Brookhaven?

Most homeowners in Brookhaven pay between $850 and $1,250 for gutter installation, depending on material and yard size.

What is the cheapest gutter material?

Chain link is the most affordable fencing option at $15-$25 per linear foot installed. Wood privacy fencing is the next most affordable at $25-$50 per linear foot.

How long does a wood gutter last?

A well-maintained wood gutter typically lasts 15-20 years. Regular staining and sealing every 2-3 years can significantly extend its lifespan. Cedar and redwood last longer than pine.
