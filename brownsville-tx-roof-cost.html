Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new roof cost in Brownsville?

Most homeowners in Brownsville pay between $6,300 and $62,100 for a complete roof replacement depending on roof size and materials.

Why do roofing quotes vary so much?

Roofing estimates vary depending on labor assumptions, material brands, flashing work, ventilation upgrades, and warranty coverage included in the proposal.

Should I always choose the lowest roofing quote?

Not always. Lower bids sometimes exclude items like flashing, ventilation upgrades, or disposal costs that appear later as change orders.

Are there special roofing requirements in Brownsville for hurricanes?

Yes. Brownsville is in a hurricane-prone area and local building codes typically require enhanced wind uplift ratings and specific fastening patterns. Your contractor should be familiar with local wind-zone requirements.
