HVAC in Buffalo: what locals should know

🌡

Climate and your HVAC

Buffalo homeowners rely heavily on heating from November through March. Proper furnace sizing matters — an oversized unit cycles too frequently and wastes energy.

⚙

System recommendation

For Buffalo homes, consider a dual-fuel system: a heat pump for efficient heating above 35°F and a gas furnace for the coldest days. It can cut heating costs 20-30% compared to gas-only.

📅

Best time to buy

Buffalo HVAC contractors are busiest December through February for heating and June through August for cooling. Book your replacement during the shoulder seasons for better pricing and faster scheduling.

💡

Local tip

Buffalo homeowners should consider a programmable or smart thermostat with their new system. Setting back temperatures while away or sleeping can save 10-15% on heating bills.
