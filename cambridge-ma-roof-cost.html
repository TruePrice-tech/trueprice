Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new roof cost in Cambridge?

Most homeowners in Cambridge pay between $7,250 and $71,400 for a complete roof replacement depending on roof size and materials.

Why do roofing quotes vary so much?

Roofing estimates vary depending on labor assumptions, material brands, flashing work, ventilation upgrades, and warranty coverage included in the proposal.

Should I always choose the lowest roofing quote?

Not always. Lower bids sometimes exclude items like flashing, ventilation upgrades, or disposal costs that appear later as change orders.

My home in Cambridge is older. Does that affect the cost?

Often yes. Homes in Cambridge average around 52 years old. Older roofs may need additional decking repair, updated ventilation, or code-required upgrades that add to the base replacement cost.
