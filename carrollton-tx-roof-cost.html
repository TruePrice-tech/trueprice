Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new roof cost in Carrollton?

Most homeowners in Carrollton pay between $6,300 and $62,100 for a complete roof replacement depending on roof size and materials.

Why do roofing quotes vary so much?

Roofing estimates vary depending on labor assumptions, material brands, flashing work, ventilation upgrades, and warranty coverage included in the proposal.

Should I always choose the lowest roofing quote?

Not always. Lower bids sometimes exclude items like flashing, ventilation upgrades, or disposal costs that appear later as change orders.

Does hail damage affect roofing costs in Carrollton?

Yes. Carrollton is in a high hail-risk area. After major storms, contractor demand spikes and wait times can stretch to weeks. Consider impact-resistant (Class 4) shingles, which may also qualify for insurance discounts.

My home in Carrollton is older. Does that affect the cost?

Often yes. Homes in Carrollton average around 36 years old. Older roofs may need additional decking repair, updated ventilation, or code-required upgrades that add to the base replacement cost.
