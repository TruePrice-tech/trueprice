Carson City hvac: answers to common questions

How long does a new HVAC system last?

A properly maintained system lasts 15-20 years. Air conditioners and heat pumps last 12-17 years, while gas furnaces can reach 20-25 years with annual maintenance.

How long does HVAC installation take in Carson City?

A standard residential HVAC replacement takes 1-2 days. Complex jobs involving ductwork modification or system-type changes can take 3-5 days.

How much does a new HVAC system cost in Carson City?

Most homeowners in Carson City pay between $4,000 and $14,000 for a new HVAC system, depending on equipment type, efficiency rating, and home size.

Contractor market picture. Carson City's moderate growth keeps the contractor pipeline moving without the extreme backlogs seen in booming metros. Timing considerations. The ideal window for HVAC work in Carson City is late fall through early spring. Contractors are more available and some offer 5-10% off-season discounts.

What drives cost. Red flags to watch for. Check that any Carson City contractor doing HVAC replacement carries both general liability insurance ($1M minimum) and workers' compensation.
