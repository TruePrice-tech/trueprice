Plumbing in Castle Rock: what locals should know

💧

Water quality

The water in Castle Rock is on the harder side. You may notice mineral spots on fixtures. For water heaters, this means flushing the tank annually is essential to prevent sediment buildup.

❄

Freeze risk

Frozen pipes happen in Castle Rock most winters. The most vulnerable spots are pipes in exterior walls, unheated basements, and garages. Pipe insulation is cheap ($0.50-1/ft) and prevents expensive burst-pipe repairs.

🔧

Material recommendation

PEX piping is the top recommendation for repiping in Castle Rock. It's flexible, resists freezing better than rigid pipes (it expands slightly), and typically costs 30-40% less than copper to install.

💡

Local tip

Many Castle Rock homes built in the 1970s-80s have polybutylene (gray plastic) water pipes, which are prone to unexpected failures. If your home has them, most plumbers recommend proactive repiping before a catastrophic leak.
