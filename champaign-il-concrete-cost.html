Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a concrete driveway cost in Champaign?

Most homeowners in Champaign pay between $6,000 and $12,850 for a concrete driveway, depending on size and finish type.

Is stamped concrete worth the extra cost?

Stamped concrete costs 30-50% more than plain concrete but can mimic the look of pavers, brick, or natural stone at a fraction of the cost. It adds significant curb appeal and value.

How long does a concrete driveway last?

A properly installed and sealed concrete driveway typically lasts 25-30 years. Regular sealing every 2-3 years and prompt crack repair can extend its lifespan further.
