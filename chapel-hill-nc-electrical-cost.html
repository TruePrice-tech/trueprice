What Chapel Hill residents ask about electrical

How much does it cost to add an outlet?

Adding a standard outlet costs $150-$300 if wiring can be run through accessible spaces. If walls need to be opened or a new circuit is required, expect $300-$600 per outlet.

How much does a whole-house generator cost in Chapel Hill?

A whole-house standby generator costs $5,000-$15,000 installed in Chapel Hill, depending on wattage (14kW-24kW), fuel type (natural gas or propane), and the transfer switch configuration.

Do I need a permit for electrical work in Chapel Hill?

Most Chapel Hill jurisdictions require permits for new circuits, panel upgrades, and any work beyond simple fixture swaps. Your electrician should handle permitting and schedule the inspection.
