Questions Charleston homeowners ask about electrical

Do I need a permit for electrical work in Charleston?

Most Charleston jurisdictions require permits for new circuits, panel upgrades, and any work beyond simple fixture swaps. Your electrician should handle permitting and schedule the inspection.

How do I know if my home needs rewiring?

Warning signs include flickering lights, warm outlets, burning smells, frequently tripping breakers, two-prong outlets throughout, and a panel with fuses instead of breakers.

When should I upgrade my electrical panel?

Upgrade if your panel uses fuses instead of breakers, you are adding major appliances (EV charger, heat pump, hot tub), breakers trip frequently, or your home has less than 200-amp service.
