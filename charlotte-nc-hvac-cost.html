HVAC questions for Charlotte homeowners

Should I replace my AC and furnace at the same time?

Replacing both at once is usually more cost-effective. Matched systems run more efficiently, and you save on labor since the contractor is already on-site.

Do I need a permit for HVAC replacement in Charlotte?

Yes. Most Charlotte jurisdictions require a mechanical permit for HVAC replacement. Your contractor should pull the permit and schedule the inspection.

When is the best time to replace HVAC in Charlotte?

Off-peak seasons offer the best pricing and availability in Charlotte. Spring and fall are typically 10-20% cheaper than emergency summer or winter replacements.
