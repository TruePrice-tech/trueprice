Roof Replacement Cost in Charlotte NC

The average roof replacement cost in Charlotte typically ranges between $9,000 and $18,000 depending on roof size, material, pitch, and complexity.

Typical Charlotte Roofing Prices

  • Architectural shingles: $500–$750 per square
  • Metal roofing: $1,000–$1,600 per square
  • Tile roofing: $1,500–$3,000 per square

Charlotte Roof Replacement Example

A typical 2,200 sq ft Charlotte home may cost:

  • Low estimate: $11,000
  • Fair range: $13,000 – $16,000
  • High estimate: $19,000+
