SEER and AFUE Targets for Chicago Homeowners

Cooling efficiency target

16+ SEER

16 SEER is the sensible Chicago minimum because heating, not cooling, dominates your ComEd (electric) bill. The 14-to-16 SEER jump delivers modest savings here; the 16-to-20 jump delivers almost none. Put the efficiency budget toward the furnace or a cold-climate heat pump instead.

Heating efficiency target

96%+ AFUE

Chicago's heavy heating load makes 95-96% AFUE the Chicago minimum on any new gas furnace. At ComEd (electric)'s $1.10/therm rate, the 80-to-96% AFUE jump saves $240-$448 annually in a typical Chicago home. What the Chicago Department of Buildings permit actually covers.
