Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a fence cost in Colorado Springs?

Most homeowners in Colorado Springs pay between $4,050 and $11,650 for fence installation, depending on material and yard size.

What is the cheapest fence material?

Chain link is the most affordable fencing option at $15-$25 per linear foot installed. Wood privacy fencing is the next most affordable at $25-$50 per linear foot.

How long does a wood fence last?

A well-maintained wood fence typically lasts 15-20 years. Regular staining and sealing every 2-3 years can significantly extend its lifespan. Cedar and redwood last longer than pine.

About fence installation in Colorado Springs, CO

Why pricing varies in Colorado Springs. Labor rates here track within a few points of the national average, so most of the price variation comes from material costs and local market dynamics rather than wage differentials. For fence installation specifically, Colorado Springs sits within a few points of the national midpoint on our cost model.

Local conditions. Colorado Springs has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Cedar, vinyl, and metal all work; set posts below the local frost line (36-48 inches) to prevent heaving.

Permits, codes, and licensing. Most fence installation projects in Colorado Springs require a permit; confirm with the local building department before signing.

Contractor market. With a population of 439,886, the local market has enough contractors to drive real competition on price, but you should still get three quotes. Colorado Springs's rapid recent growth has tightened the contractor market — expect longer lead times and less negotiating room than smaller markets.
