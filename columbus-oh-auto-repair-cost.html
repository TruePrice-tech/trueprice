Columbus Vehicle Inspections and Warranty Protections

Franklin County participates in Ohio's E-Check vehicle emissions testing program. Most vehicles between 4 and 25 model years old must pass a biennial OBD-II scan at a state-certified testing station. The fee is $19.50 per test. There is no separate annual safety inspection. Vehicles that fail the E-Check must undergo documented repairs and retest. The state's repair-cost waiver provision caps mandatory expenditures at $450 before a conditional pass can be granted for remaining low-level emission faults.

Ohio's Lemon Law provides coverage for new vehicles with unresolved defects reported within the first 12 months or 18,000 miles of ownership. The Ohio Consumer Sales Practices Act extends protection against deceptive and unconscionable auto-repair billing. Shops are obligated to furnish written estimates upon request and may not exceed the approved amount without explicit customer authorization.
