How to compare roofing quotes correctly

Homeowners often compare quotes by headline price alone. That is the fastest way to make a costly mistake. The better approach is to compare the exact scope of work. Ask whether each quote includes tear off, disposal, underlayment, flashing replacement, ridge ventilation, drip edge, permit assumptions, and any decking replacement terms.

One contractor may price lower simply because they omitted critical items that will show up later as change orders. Another may look expensive at first but include stronger materials, better workmanship coverage, and more complete flashing and ventilation work.
