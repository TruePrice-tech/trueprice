# Kitchen Remodel Pricing Reference (2026)

Build-agent reference for the Woogoro kitchen vertical. All numbers are 2026 installed unless otherwise noted. Sources cited inline.

---

## 1. Four Remodel Tiers (US national averages, 2026)

| Tier | Total project | $/sqft installed | Typical kitchen size | What's included |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Cosmetic / Refresh** | $5,000 - $25,000 | $75 - $150 | 100-180 sqft | Paint, hardware swap, refacing or repainting cabinets, new sink/faucet, new countertop only OR new appliances only, no layout change, no permits |
| **Mid-Range** | $25,000 - $75,000 | $150 - $350 | 150-200 sqft | New stock or low semi-custom cabinets, quartz or low granite, mid-tier appliance package, new flooring, new lighting, same layout, light electrical, permits |
| **Upscale** | $75,000 - $150,000 | $350 - $600 | 180-250 sqft | Semi-custom or low custom cabinets, premium quartz/quartzite, Bosch/KitchenAid premium or Thermador entry, layout changes, island add, full electrical and plumbing rework, designer fee |
| **Luxury** | $150,000 - $400,000+ | $600 - $1,500+ | 250-500 sqft | Full custom cabinetry (Wood-Mode, Plain & Fancy), exotic stone, Sub-Zero/Wolf/Miele package, structural changes (load-bearing wall removal), butler's pantry, architect, high-end lighting design |

National median 2026: $27,000-$35,000. Most projects fall $15k-$75k. Source: HomeGuide, Fixr, Angi, Block Renovation 2026 data.

**Hard ceiling note:** Ultra-custom NYC/SF/LA/Aspen projects routinely exceed $750-$1,500/sqft. A 200 sqft kitchen at this level = $150k-$300k.

## 2. Cabinet Pricing (the single biggest line item: 30-40% of materials, 25-35% of total project)

Cabinets are quoted **per linear foot installed** (LF). A typical 10x10 kitchen has ~20 linear feet of cabinets.

## 6. Labor Breakdown

Labor = 20-35% of total. On a $50k kitchen, expect $10k-$17.5k labor:

|---|---|---|

| Framing | $500 - $3,000 | If walls modified |
| Drywall + tape + texture | $1,500 - $4,000 | $2-$4/sf |

| Plumbing (rough + finish) | $1,500 - $5,000 | $75-$150/hr; relocating sink $1,000-$3,000 |
| HVAC modifications | $500 - $3,000 | If venting hood through roof or moving duct |

| Countertop install | included in $/sqft | Templating + install |

| Free design (Home Depot, IKEA, Lowe's) | $0 - assumes you buy from them | $0 cabinet design only, no full layout/structural |

NKBA standard: 10% of total project budget for full-service kitchen design.

## 8. Permit Costs by Region (2026)

Kitchen remodels with plumbing or electrical work require a permit in essentially every US jurisdiction.

| Region | Combined kitchen permit |
|---|---|
| Rural/small town | $200 - $500 |
| Mid-size city (Chicago, Houston, Phoenix) | $500 - $1,500 |
| NYC | $1,500 - $5,000 + DOB filing fees, often $3k-$8k incl expediter |
| Los Angeles / SF Bay | $1,500 - $5,000 + Title 24 energy compliance docs ($500-$1,500) |
| Boston | $500 - $2,000 |
| Miami-Dade | $400 - $1,500 + 40-yr recertification triggers if older bldg |
| HOA / co-op fees | $250 - $2,500 application + alteration agreement |

Stringent CA permitting alone adds $3,000-$8,000 to projects (Sweeten 2026).

Plumbing permit alone: $50-$500. Electrical permit alone: $50-$500.

## 12. Quick Sanity-Check Heuristics for Estimator

- **10% rule:** Total kitchen budget should be ~10-15% of home value. A $400k home → $40-$60k kitchen.
- **Cabinets ≈ 1/3:** If contractor's bid shows cabinets <20% or >50% of total, ask why.
- **Linear foot rule:** Total project ÷ linear feet of cabinet ≈ $1,500-$3,500/LF mid-range, $4k-$8k/LF luxury.
- **Per-sqft floor:** Anything below $100/sqft is cosmetic only; below $50/sqft is suspect.
- **Per-sqft ceiling:** Above $1,500/sqft = ultra-luxury or someone's overcharging.
