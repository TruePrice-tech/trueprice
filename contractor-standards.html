TruePrice Contractor Standards

We don't sell leads. We don't let anyone pay for placement. Every contractor on TruePrice earns their listing.

TruePrice exists to help homeowners and car owners make informed decisions. Our contractor directory is built on trust, not ad spend. These standards ensure that every listed business meets a baseline of professionalism and transparency.

Listing Tiers

Basic Listing

Free
  • Available to any contractor or repair shop
  • Business name, phone, service area, services offered
  • Marked as "unverified" until standards are met
  • No priority placement
  • Must agree to TruePrice Terms of Service

Verified Listing

Free — earned

Requirements

  • Valid state/local contractor license (or ASE certification for auto repair)
  • Proof of general liability insurance (minimum $1M)
  • Active for 2+ years (business registration or equivalent)
  • No unresolved BBB complaints
  • Minimum 3.5 star average on Google Reviews (10+ reviews)

Benefits

  • "Verified" badge on listing
  • Appears above unverified listings in search results
  • Eligible to appear in quote analysis results

Featured Listing

Paid — must be Verified first

Requirements

  • Must hold Verified status (all requirements above)
  • Must maintain a 4.0+ star Google Review average
  • Must agree to the TruePrice Transparency Pledge
  • Subject to periodic review; featured status can be revoked

Benefits

  • Priority placement in directory search
  • Appears on quote analysis result pages for your service area
  • Company logo displayed on listing
  • Link to your website
  • "Featured" badge

Pricing: Contact us

TruePrice Transparency Pledge

Contractors with Featured listings agree to the following:

  • Provide itemized quotes (labor and materials separated)
  • State labor rates explicitly
  • Include warranty terms in every quote
  • No same-day signing pressure or "today only" pricing
  • Include permit costs if applicable
  • Respond to customer disputes within 5 business days
  • Allow TruePrice to display anonymized quote data for benchmarking (no customer PII)

Removal and Suspension Policy

TruePrice reserves the right to remove or downgrade any listing if:

  • License or insurance lapses
  • Google Review average drops below 3.0
  • Multiple consumer complaints are filed
  • Contractor violates the Transparency Pledge
  • Contractor engages in fake review activity
  • Contractor misrepresents services, pricing, or credentials

Removed contractors may reapply after 6 months.

Auto Repair Shop Standards

The same tier structure applies to auto repair shops. In addition to the general requirements above, the following apply:

  • ASE certification (at least one certified mechanic on staff) for Verified status
  • Valid state motor vehicle repair license where required
  • Itemized quotes showing parts (OEM/aftermarket/reman), labor hours, and labor rate
  • Posted shop labor rate
  • Parts and labor warranty terms disclosed

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to pay to be listed?
No. Basic and Verified listings are free. Featured placement requires Verified status and a monthly fee.
How long does verification take?
Typically 3-5 business days after submitting documentation.
Can I lose my Verified status?
Yes. If your license, insurance, or review average falls below our standards, your listing will be downgraded to Basic until resolved.
Do you sell my leads?
No. Homeowners contact you directly. We never share contractor information with third parties.
