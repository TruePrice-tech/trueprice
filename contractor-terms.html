Last updated: April 27, 2026

This Contractor Participation Agreement (this “Agreement”) governs your participation as a contractor, repair shop, healthcare provider, attorney, moving company, or other service provider (“Contractor,” “you,” or “your”) in any directory, listing, scoring, or referral feature operated by Woogoro LLC, a South Carolina limited liability company (“Woogoro,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), with its principal place of business at 17064 Laurelmont Court, Fort Mill, SC 29707. By submitting an application through our contractor signup flow at woogoro.com/contractor-signup.html, or by accepting placement in any Woogoro contractor listing, you agree to this Agreement. If you do not agree, do not submit an application and do not accept placement.

This Agreement is supplemental to and incorporates by reference our general Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Where a conflict arises between this Agreement and the general Terms with respect to your contractor relationship with Woogoro, this Agreement controls.

1. Opt-In Listing Only

Woogoro does not list contractors without their affirmative opt-in. We do not scrape, ingest, or auto-populate contractor listings from third-party sources, lead-broker feeds, or business registries. Your listing exists in our directory only because you submitted an application and met the criteria for the tier you applied for. If you discover a Woogoro listing for your business that you did not create or authorize, contact hello@woogoro.com immediately for removal.

2. Information You Submit

By submitting your application, you represent and warrant that the following information is accurate, complete, and current as of the date of submission, and you authorize Woogoro to display the items marked “public” below in your public listing:

  • Business name and contact information — name, business email, phone, website (public).
  • Business location — service city and state (public). Street address is not displayed unless you opt in separately.
  • Services offered — the list of service categories you select during signup (public).
  • State license number — where required by your trade and state (public, displayed verbatim as you entered it).
  • Insurance carrier and coverage amount as you self-reported — displayed publicly only as “Self-reported insurance on file (last reviewed YYYY-MM-DD)” or similar non-attesting language. The reviewed-on date reflects the date Woogoro received your document; it is not a representation that coverage is currently in force.
  • Years in business and business type — as you self-reported (public).
  • Score badge — an algorithmic tier (e.g. Featured, Self-reported, etc.) determined by Woogoro under the methodology described in Section 4 (public).

Internal-only data (such as document images uploaded for license or insurance review, AI-generated review summaries, and signup IP/timestamps) is retained per the Privacy Policy and is not displayed publicly.

3. Self-Reported Information; No Verification by Woogoro

The information you submit, including your license number, insurance carrier, coverage amounts, expiration dates, and years in business, is self-reported by you. Woogoro does not independently verify the accuracy or current validity of this information. Any document review performed by Woogoro is limited to a one-time check of the document image you uploaded on the date of submission and reflects only what was visible in that image. Woogoro does not (a) confirm with state licensing boards that a license is currently active or unrestricted, (b) confirm with insurance carriers that coverage is currently in force, or (c) re-verify any information after the initial review. You agree to keep your information current and to notify Woogoro promptly at hello@woogoro.com of any material change.

You represent and warrant that all information you submit is true and accurate at the time of submission, that any license number listed is your active state license, and that any insurance certificate uploaded reflects coverage in force as of the date of upload. You agree to indemnify Woogoro for any third-party claim arising from your submission of inaccurate, expired, or fraudulent license or insurance information.

4. Scoring Methodology Acknowledgment

Score badges, tier labels, and ranking positions displayed on Woogoro contractor listings are algorithmic outputs based on factors including (without limitation): completeness of self-reported information, presence of a state license number, presence of an insurance certificate on file, scope items confirmed in the contractor pledge, contractor-side red-flag signals, and other factors Woogoro determines from time to time at its sole discretion. The methodology is described in general terms at /methodology.html and may be updated by Woogoro at any time without notice. You acknowledge that:

  • Score badges and tier labels are statements of opinion under New York Gen. Bus. Law § 349/350 and equivalent state laws, not statements of fact.
  • Two contractors with similar self-reported inputs may receive different scores due to methodology updates, presence or absence of contractor pledge items, or completeness of submitted documents.
  • Score, tier, and ranking placement may change at any time as the methodology, your inputs, or your activity changes, without prior notice.
  • You have no right to a particular score, tier, ranking position, or featured placement.

After removal, Woogoro may retain a minimal record of the application (email, date submitted, date removed) for fraud-prevention and re-application purposes only.
