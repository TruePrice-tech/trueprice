DIY saves 60-70% on cost but takes 3-5x longer and usually looks less polished. Hire a pro for exteriors, high ceilings, detailed trim work, or any spray application. DIY works fine for single interior rooms.

Professional cabinet painting costs $3,000-$7,000 for an average kitchen. The process includes removing doors, degreasing, sanding, priming, spraying 2-3 coats, and reinstalling hardware.
