Questions Deerfield Beach homeowners ask about siding

What siding material lasts the longest?

Fiber cement (James Hardie) lasts 40-50 years with minimal maintenance. Vinyl lasts 25-40 years. Engineered wood lasts 20-30 years. Natural wood lasts 20-40 years but requires repainting every 5-7 years.

How do I know my siding needs replacing?

Replace when you see warping, buckling, cracks, or holes; soft spots indicating rot; bubbling paint that keeps returning; high energy bills from poor insulation; or mold and mildew that will not clean away.

How much does siding replacement cost in Deerfield Beach?

Siding replacement in Deerfield Beach costs $6,000-$18,000 for an average home. Vinyl is the most affordable ($4-$8/sqft installed), while fiber cement ($8-$14/sqft) and wood ($10-$18/sqft) cost more.

What is the most energy-efficient siding?

Insulated vinyl siding (with foam backing) adds R-2 to R-5 insulation. Fiber cement over rigid foam sheathing provides better thermal performance. The biggest energy gains come from fixing air leaks during installation.
