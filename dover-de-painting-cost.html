What Dover residents ask about painting

What should I look for in a contractor quote?

A professional quote breaks out labor, materials, permits, and cleanup separately. It includes a timeline, payment schedule, warranty terms, and the contractor's license number.

Do I need a permit for exterior painting in Dover?

Most exterior painting projects in Dover require a permit. Your contractor should pull the permit and schedule the inspection. Never pull permits in your own name.

What are common red flags in contractor quotes?

Red flags include: no line-item detail, same-day signing pressure, request for more than 30% upfront, no license or insurance verification, and verbal-only warranties.

When is the best time to schedule this work in Dover?

Off-peak seasons offer better pricing and availability. In Dover, scheduling during the shoulder season saves 10-20% versus peak-demand months.

How long does the project typically take?

Timeline depends on scope and complexity. Simple projects take 1-3 days; larger jobs can run 1-4 weeks. Weather, permits, and material delivery affect scheduling.

Local conditions. Dover has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Dover's mix of humidity, moderate UV, and seasonal temperature swings means exterior paint faces all three major degradation pathways. 100% acrylic latex with mildew-resistant primer is the standard — expect 7-10 year repaint cycles with proper prep.
