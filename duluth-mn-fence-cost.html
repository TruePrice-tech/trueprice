Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a fence cost in Duluth?

Most homeowners in Duluth pay between $4,550 and $13,150 for fence installation, depending on material and yard size.

What is the cheapest fence material?

Chain link is the most affordable fencing option at $15-$25 per linear foot installed. Wood privacy fencing is the next most affordable at $25-$50 per linear foot.

How long does a wood fence last?

A well-maintained wood fence typically lasts 15-20 years. Regular staining and sealing every 2-3 years can significantly extend its lifespan. Cedar and redwood last longer than pine.

About fence installation in Duluth, MN

Why pricing varies in Duluth. Labor rates here run roughly 35% above the national average per BLS metro data, which is the single biggest driver of how this city's pricing differs from the national midpoint. For fence installation specifically, our cost-multiplier model puts Duluth at roughly 6% above the national midpoint.

Local conditions. Duluth has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Set posts 48-60 inches deep below frost line; cedar and metal both work.

Permits, codes, and licensing. Most fence installation projects in Duluth require a permit; confirm with the local building department before signing.

Contractor market. At 86,128 residents, the local pool is moderate; expect 4-6 active contractors for most standard residential work. Duluth's stable population means contractor capacity rarely tightens, so off-season pricing (typically Nov-Feb) can save 10-15% on non-emergency work.
