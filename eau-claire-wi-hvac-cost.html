Eau Claire hvac: answers to common questions

What is the difference between a heat pump and a furnace?

A heat pump moves heat using refrigerant and handles both heating and cooling. A furnace burns fuel to create heat and requires a separate AC unit for cooling.

Should I replace my AC and furnace at the same time?

Replacing both at once is usually more cost-effective. Matched systems run more efficiently, and you save on labor since the contractor is already on-site.

Do I need a permit for HVAC replacement in Eau Claire?

Yes. Most Eau Claire jurisdictions require a mechanical permit for HVAC replacement. Your contractor should pull the permit and schedule the inspection.
