Electrical Work Cost Guide (2026)

By Geoff Lane, founder of Woogoro · Updated April 2026

Compare panel upgrade, rewiring, EV charger installation, and generator costs across 1,000+ U.S. cities.

Electrical work costs $200 to $14,800 depending on scope. Small repairs run $200 to $500, panel upgrades average $2,500 to $4,500, and full home rewiring costs $5,000 to $15,000. Licensed electricians charge $75 to $150 per hour plus permits in 2026.

What Should an Electrical Quote Include?

  • Permits and inspections
  • Electrical panel and breakers
  • Wiring (Romex or conduit)
  • Grounding system
  • Conduit and raceway
  • Trenching (if needed for generators/EV chargers)
  • Testing and commissioning
  • Cleanup and disposal
  • Warranty (parts + labor)
  • Code compliance review
  • Meter upgrade coordination
