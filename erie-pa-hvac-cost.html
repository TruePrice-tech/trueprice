Questions Erie homeowners ask about hvac

Do I need a permit for HVAC replacement in Erie?

Yes. Most Erie jurisdictions require a mechanical permit for HVAC replacement. Your contractor should pull the permit and schedule the inspection.

When is the best time to replace HVAC in Erie?

Off-peak seasons offer the best pricing and availability in Erie. Spring and fall are typically 10-20% cheaper than emergency summer or winter replacements.

What SEER rating should I choose?

16 SEER is the sweet spot for most homeowners. Higher ratings save more on energy but have longer payback periods. In hot climates, 18+ SEER pays back faster.
