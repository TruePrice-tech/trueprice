Climate and your HVAC

In Everett, heating accounts for 50-60% of annual energy costs. Upgrading from an old furnace to a modern high-efficiency model is one of the best investments a homeowner can make.

⚙

System recommendation

In Everett, a high-efficiency gas furnace (95%+ AFUE) paired with a moderate-SEER AC (14-16) gives you the best value. Heating is your biggest expense, so prioritize furnace efficiency.

📅

Best time to buy

Everett HVAC contractors are busiest December through February for heating and June through August for cooling. Book your replacement during the shoulder seasons for better pricing and faster scheduling.

💡

Local tip

Everett homeowners should consider a programmable or smart thermostat with their new system. Setting back temperatures while away or sleeping can save 10-15% on heating bills.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new HVAC system cost in Everett?

Most homeowners in Everett pay between $9,478 and $27,197 for HVAC replacement, depending on system type and efficiency.

Should I replace my AC and furnace at the same time?

Replacing both at once is usually more cost-effective than separate replacements. Matched systems run more efficiently and most contractors offer bundle pricing.

What SEER rating should I choose?

14 SEER is the minimum federal requirement. 16 SEER offers a good balance of efficiency and cost. 18-20+ SEER provides the best energy savings but costs significantly more upfront.
