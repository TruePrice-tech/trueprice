Plumbing questions for Farmington Hills homeowners

How much does a plumber charge per hour in Farmington Hills?

Plumbers in Farmington Hills typically charge $75-$150 per hour. Emergency and after-hours calls add a $100-$200 trip fee on top of the hourly rate.

How often should I have my drains cleaned in Farmington Hills?

Annual drain maintenance is recommended for most Farmington Hills homes. Older homes with mature trees nearby may need semi-annual service to prevent root intrusion.

Can I do plumbing repairs myself?

Simple tasks like replacing a faucet cartridge or fixing a running toilet are DIY-friendly. Anything involving supply lines, gas connections, sewer work, or permits should be left to a licensed plumber.
