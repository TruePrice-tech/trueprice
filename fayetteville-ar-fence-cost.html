What Fayetteville residents ask about fencing

What is the best fence for privacy?

A 6-foot solid board (dog-ear, board-on-board, or shadowbox) fence provides the most privacy. Board-on-board is best since it has no gaps even when boards shrink. Vinyl and composite also offer full privacy panels.

How much does a new fence cost in Fayetteville?

Fence costs in Fayetteville range from $15-$35 per linear foot for wood, $20-$40 for vinyl, $15-$30 for chain link, and $25-$55 for aluminum or wrought iron, including materials and installation.

Should fence posts be set in concrete?

Yes, for most fences. Concrete-set posts are more stable and resist leaning. Some installers use compacted gravel for better drainage in wet climates, which can extend post life by reducing ground-level rot.

When is the best time to install a fence?

Late spring through early fall offers the best conditions for concrete curing and working in the ground. Avoid frozen ground and extremely wet seasons. Many contractors offer off-season discounts.

How long does fence installation take?

A standard 150-200 linear foot residential fence takes 1-3 days to install. Hillside or rocky soil adds time. Post holes need 24-48 hours to cure if set in concrete.

How long does a wood fence last in Fayetteville?

A well-maintained pressure-treated fence lasts 15-20 years in Fayetteville. Cedar holds up even longer at 15-25 years thanks to natural rot resistance. Untreated pine deteriorates quickly, typically just 5-8 years. The two highest-impact maintenance habits: apply stain or sealant every 2-3 years, and trim vegetation away from the fence base to prevent moisture buildup.

Is a Fayetteville permit needed to build a fence?

Most Fayetteville jurisdictions require permits for fences, especially those over 6 feet. Check with your local building department before starting. You must call 811 for utility locates before digging, verify property lines (consider a survey if boundaries are unclear), and check any HOA rules. Your contractor should handle permitting as part of the project.

Fayetteville's rapid growth pushes fence installation costs higher than national averages: $20-$35/ft for wood privacy, $25-$45 for vinyl, $15-$25 for chain link, and $30-$60 for ornamental metal. Local Fencing Guide for Fayetteville, AR

Fence longevity in Fayetteville depends on surviving multiple wear modes. Summer humidity drives rot; winter cold drives post heave; spring storms test hardware. Wood fences here need reliable staining every 2-3 years, and post footings deserve an extra few inches of depth for frost protection.

Many Fayetteville properties (average home age 38 years) have aging or deteriorated fences that predate current building codes. Replacement often requires updated post depth, property line verification, and sometimes a survey to confirm boundaries before new fence construction begins. Budget $300-$600 for a property survey if boundary markers are not clearly visible.
