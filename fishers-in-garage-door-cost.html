Frequently asked garage door questions

Can I insulate my existing garage door?

Yes. Retrofit insulation kits ($100-$200 DIY) add R-4 to R-8. However, adding weight to a non-insulated door may require spring adjustment. A new insulated door is often a better investment.

How much does a garage door opener cost?

A new opener installed costs $250-$600 for chain or belt drive. Smart openers with Wi-Fi and battery backup run $400-$800 installed. Replacing the opener alone takes 2-3 hours.

What is the difference between chain and belt drive openers?

Chain drive is cheaper ($150-$250) but louder. Belt drive ($200-$350) is whisper-quiet and ideal when bedrooms are above or adjacent to the garage. Both have similar reliability.

Should I repair or replace my garage door?

Repair if damage is limited to one or two panels and replacements are available. Replace if the door is over 15 years old, panels are no longer manufactured, or you want better insulation and security.

Does a new garage door increase home value?

A garage door replacement recovers 90-100% of its cost at resale, making it one of the highest-ROI home improvements. It also dramatically improves curb appeal.

What R-value garage door do I need in Fishers?

Fishers's winters demand serious insulation. An R-12 to R-16 door is the baseline for attached garages, with R-16 to R-18 recommended if you use the space as a workshop or heat it. The $200-$500 upgrade cost pays back through lower heating bills in rooms adjacent to the garage within 3-5 years.

Should I repair or replace my garage door in Fishers?

Repair makes financial sense for isolated damage: a broken spring, one or two panels, or an opener malfunction. Replace when the door exceeds 15 years, replacement panels are unavailable, you want insulation you do not currently have, or you are preparing to sell. Full replacement ($1,200-$3,000) recovers 90-100% at resale.

When is the best time to replace a garage door in Fishers?

Fishers homeowners get the best pricing and selection by scheduling garage door replacement during late spring or early fall. Moderate temperatures allow proper weatherstrip sealing and caulk curing, and you can save 5-10% versus peak season pricing. Spring demand surges from cold-weather spring failures, so plan ahead to avoid the rush.
