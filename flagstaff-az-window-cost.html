Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to replace windows in Flagstaff?

Most homeowners in Flagstaff pay between $6,650 and $20,000 for a full-home window replacement, depending on window type and quantity.

Are vinyl or wood windows better?

Vinyl windows are the most affordable and low-maintenance option. Wood windows offer a premium look and better insulation but cost more and require periodic maintenance.

How many windows does a typical home have?

Most homes have between 15 and 25 windows. A standard 3-bedroom home typically has around 15-20 windows.
