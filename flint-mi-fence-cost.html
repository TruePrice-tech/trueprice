Questions Flint homeowners ask about fencing

Who owns the fence between two properties?

Typically the homeowner who installed it. If the fence sits exactly on the property line, both neighbors share responsibility. Always get a survey before building to confirm the boundary.

What is the difference between dog-ear and flat-top fence?

Dog-ear pickets have angled cuts at the top for water runoff and a traditional look. Flat-top pickets have a clean, modern appearance but hold slightly more water, which can accelerate rot.

Is a Flint permit needed to build a fence?

Most Flint jurisdictions require a permit for fences over 6 feet tall. Many require permits for any fence. You also need to check setback requirements, HOA rules, and call 811 for utility locates before digging.

Does a fence increase property value?

A well-built fence adds 1-5% to property value depending on material and neighborhood norms. Privacy fencing adds more value in neighborhoods where it is expected. Chain link adds minimal value.

How deep should fence posts be in Flint?

In Flint's cold climate, fence posts should extend at least 6 inches below the frost line to prevent heaving. For most of Flint, this means 36-48 inches deep with 8-10 inches of compacted gravel at the bottom for drainage and concrete from there to grade. Shallow posts in freeze-prone areas will lean within 2-3 winters regardless of how well the rest of the fence is built.

Is a Flint permit needed to build a fence?

Fence permits are required in most Flint jurisdictions, particularly for heights over 6 feet. Before breaking ground: call 811 for utility locates, verify property lines (a survey costs $300-$600 if boundaries are unclear), and check HOA requirements. A professional contractor handles the permit process as part of the project.

How much does fence installation cost per foot in Flint?

Per-foot costs for fence installation in Flint: wood privacy $15-$30, vinyl $20-$40, chain link $10-$20, and aluminum or wrought iron $25-$55. A standard 150-foot backyard fence runs $2,250-$6,000 total. Always compare 3 quotes with the same scope and materials to ensure you are getting fair pricing.
