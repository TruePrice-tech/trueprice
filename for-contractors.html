Frequently asked questions

How is this different from ServiceTitan, Jobber, or Housecall Pro?
Those are full CRMs that cost $300\u2013500/mo and take weeks to set up. Woogoro Pro is focused: it writes accurate quotes from photos, fast. You can start today for $49/mo. Use it alongside your existing CRM or on its own.
How accurate are the AI-generated prices?
We use Woogoro's benchmark pricing across 12,000 cities and 20 trades \u2014 the same data homeowners see when they check if a quote is fair. The AI drafts a price range based on your city, scope, and materials. You review and adjust before sending. You're the expert. The AI just handles the paperwork.
Do you sell leads like Angi or HomeAdvisor?
Never. That's our whole point. Flat-rate subscription, you own your customer relationships, we don't resell your info. The Plus and Elite tiers include directory placement on Woogoro city pages, but homeowners see a transparent list of verified contractors \u2014 not a bidding war.
What trades do you support?
Launch trades: roofing, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, windows, siding, insulation, painting, fencing, concrete, landscaping, garage doors, solar, foundation, kitchen remodeling, gutters, auto repair, moving, and medical billing. Same 20 trades Woogoro already has pricing data for.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly subscription, cancel with one click. No long-term contracts. No cancellation fees. If you cancel in the first 30 days, you get a full refund.
What do I need to get started?
A phone, a few photos, and 5 minutes. We'll set up your profile, your business logo on the quote template, and your preferred pricing. After that, every new quote takes just a few minutes.
How fast can I send my first quote?
Most contractors send their first real quote within 10 minutes of signing up. The onboarding walks you through a practice quote using photos from a past job. By the time you're done, you'll be ready for live ones.
