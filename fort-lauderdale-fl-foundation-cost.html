Local conditions. Fort Lauderdale has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Foundation problems in Fort Lauderdale are primarily driven by soil moisture cycling — expansive clay soils swell when wet and shrink when dry, creating the settlement and heave patterns that crack foundations.
