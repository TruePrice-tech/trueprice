Foundation Repair Cost Guide (2026)

By Geoff Lane, founder of Woogoro · Updated April 2026

Compare foundation repair costs by repair type and city across 1,000+ U.S. locations.

Foundation repair costs $500 to $26,000 depending on damage severity. Minor crack repair runs $500 to $1,500. Pier installation averages $1,800 to $3,000 per pier, with 6 to 12 piers typical per project. Full underpinning ranges $15,000 to $26,000. Most homeowners spend $5,000 to $10,000.

What Should a Foundation Repair Quote Include?

  • Inspection and engineering report
  • Steel push or helical piers with brackets
  • Excavation and backfill
  • Waterproofing and sealing
  • Drainage system if needed
  • Post-repair monitoring
  • Transferable warranty
  • Permits and inspections
  • Landscaping repair
