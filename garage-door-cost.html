Garage Door Installation Cost Guide (2026)

By Geoff Lane, founder of Woogoro · Updated April 2026

Compare garage door prices by size, material, and city across 1,000+ U.S. locations.

Garage door costs $950 to $4,000 installed. Single-car doors average $950 to $1,800, double-car doors run $1,500 to $3,500, and custom wood or high-end insulated doors exceed $4,000. Opener installation adds $250 to $650. Labor averages $200 to $400 per door in 2026.

What Should a Garage Door Quote Include?

  • Old door removal and disposal
  • New door, tracks, and hardware
  • Torsion or extension springs
  • Garage door opener with remotes
  • Weatherstripping and bottom seal
  • Insulation (if applicable)
  • Safety sensors and auto-reverse
  • Wireless keypad
  • Warranty (parts and labor)
  • Permits if required
