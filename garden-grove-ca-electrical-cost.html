Garden Grove electrical: answers to common questions

Adding a standard outlet costs $150-$300 if wiring can be run through accessible spaces. If walls need to be opened or a new circuit is required, expect $300-$600 per outlet.

A whole-house standby generator costs $5,000-$15,000 installed in Garden Grove, depending on wattage (14kW-24kW), fuel type (natural gas or propane), and the transfer switch configuration.

Most Garden Grove jurisdictions require permits for new circuits, panel upgrades, and any work beyond simple fixture swaps. Your electrician should handle permitting and schedule the inspection.
