Frequently asked hvac questions

Should I replace my AC and furnace at the same time?

Replacing both at once is usually more cost-effective. Matched systems run more efficiently, and you save on labor since the contractor is already on-site.

Do I need a permit for HVAC replacement in Garland?

Yes. Most Garland jurisdictions require a mechanical permit for HVAC replacement. Your contractor should pull the permit and schedule the inspection.

Best time of year to replace HVAC in Garland?

Off-peak seasons offer the best pricing and availability in Garland. Spring and fall are typically 10-20% cheaper than emergency summer or winter replacements.
