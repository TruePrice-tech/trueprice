Frequently asked gutter questions

How much do new gutters cost in Grand Rapids?

Gutters in Grand Rapids cost $6-$15 per linear foot for aluminum seamless, $8-$20 for steel, and $15-$30 for copper. A typical home (150-200 linear feet) runs $1,200-$3,000 for aluminum.

What causes gutters to pull away from the house?

Common causes: rotted fascia board behind the gutter, failed or spaced-too-far-apart hangers, ice and snow weight, clogged gutters holding heavy water, and improper original installation.

Do gutters protect the foundation?

Yes. Gutters prevent concentrated roof runoff from eroding soil next to the foundation and saturating the ground. Homes without gutters have 3-5x more foundation moisture problems than homes with properly functioning gutter systems.

About gutter installation in Grand Rapids, MI

Why pricing varies in Grand Rapids. BLS data puts Grand Rapids's skilled trade wages near the national midpoint. The local pricing you see is shaped more by material costs, permit fees, and competition than by labor rate differences. For gutter installation specifically, our cost-multiplier model puts Grand Rapids at roughly 6% below the national midpoint.

Local conditions. Grand Rapids has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Ice dams are the primary gutter concern in Grand Rapids. Heated gutter cables ($400-800 installed per 50 feet) prevent the ice backup that tears gutters off eaves and drives water behind siding. Ice-and-water shield membrane on the eave edge (6+ feet up from the gutter line) provides the secondary defense. Seamless aluminum gutters are the Grand Rapids standard: $6-12 per linear foot installed. Copper ($20-40/ft) and zinc ($15-25/ft) offer 50+ year lifespans but at 3-4x the cost. For most homeowners, aluminum with a quality finish provides the best value at 20-30 years before replacement.

Permits, codes, and licensing. Most gutter installation projects in Grand Rapids require a permit; confirm with the local building department before signing.
