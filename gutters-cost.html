What Affects Gutter Cost?

Material

Aluminum seamless is the most popular choice (80%+ of installations). Copper is premium. Vinyl is cheapest but less durable.

Home Size and Stories

A typical home needs 100-200 linear feet. Two-story homes cost 20% more due to ladder/scaffolding work.

Gutter Guards

Leaf guards add $7-$20 per linear foot but reduce maintenance. Premium brands (LeafGuard, Leaf Filter) cost more.

Fascia Repair

If fascia boards are rotted, expect $5-$15 per linear foot for replacement before gutter installation.

What Should Be Included in a Gutter Quote

A complete gutter installation quote should include these 12 items:

  • Old gutter removal and disposal
  • New gutter installation
  • Downspouts (typically 1 per 30-40 LF)
  • Hidden hangers/brackets
  • End caps and corners
  • Outlets and connectors
  • Splash blocks or extensions
  • Fascia board repair (if needed)
  • Gutter guards/leaf protection (optional)
  • Cleanup and disposal
  • Warranty (product + labor)
  • Sealant at joints
