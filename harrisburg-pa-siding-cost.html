Frequently Asked Questions

How much does new siding cost in Harrisburg?

Most homeowners in Harrisburg pay between $5,850 and $20,400 for siding replacement, depending on material and home size.

Is fiber cement siding worth the extra cost?

Fiber cement (like James Hardie) costs more than vinyl but lasts 30-50 years, resists fire and termites, and adds more resale value. It is a popular mid-range upgrade.

How long does siding replacement take?

Most siding projects take 1-2 weeks depending on home size and material. Vinyl installs fastest, while fiber cement and wood take longer.

About siding replacement in Harrisburg, PA

Why pricing varies in Harrisburg. Labor rates here track within a few points of the national average, so most of the price variation comes from material costs and local market dynamics rather than wage differentials. For siding replacement specifically, Harrisburg sits within a few points of the national midpoint on our cost model.

Local conditions. Harrisburg has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Freeze-thaw cycles in Harrisburg test siding at every seam and penetration. Water infiltrates behind panels, freezes, expands, and forces siding away from sheathing. Fiber cement and insulated vinyl handle this cycle best — standard vinyl can crack in deep cold, and wood expands and contracts enough to pop nails. Siding costs in Harrisburg vary dramatically by material: vinyl ($4-8/sqft installed), fiber cement ($8-14/sqft), engineered wood ($9-15/sqft), cedar ($10-18/sqft), and stone veneer ($15-30/sqft). For a 2,000 sqft exterior, that's $8,000-60,000 — material choice is the single biggest pricing decision.

Permits, codes, and licensing. Most siding replacement projects in Harrisburg require a permit; confirm with the local building department before signing.

Contractor market. At 49,188 residents, the local pool is moderate; expect 4-6 active contractors for most standard residential work. Harrisburg has seen steady growth, which means a healthy supply of established contractors with multi-year track records.
