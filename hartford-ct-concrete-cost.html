Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a concrete driveway cost in Hartford?

Most homeowners in Hartford pay between $5,150 and $11,000 for a concrete driveway, depending on size and finish type.

Is stamped concrete worth the extra cost?

Stamped concrete costs 30-50% more than plain concrete but can mimic the look of pavers, brick, or natural stone at a fraction of the cost. It adds significant curb appeal and value.

How long does a concrete driveway last?

A properly installed and sealed concrete driveway typically lasts 25-30 years. Regular sealing every 2-3 years and prompt crack repair can extend its lifespan further.

Why pricing varies in Hartford. Local conditions. Hartford has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Air-entrained mix (5-7%) is mandatory; sealers extend service life on driveways exposed to road salt.

Contractor market. With a population of 125,017, the local market has enough contractors to drive real competition on price, but you should still get three quotes.
