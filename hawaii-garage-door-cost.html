Garage Door Replacement Cost in Hawaii (2026)

Garage door replacements in Hawaii typically run $1,900–$6,200 for a 16x7 single-bay or 18x7 double-bay steel-insulated garage door + opener replacement, with steel-insulated panel the dominant residential choice and 130 mph ASCE 7-22 design wind speed driving DASMA wind-pressure rating selection. Hawaii's combination of persistent Pacific salt-spray on every coastal exposure driving stainless-hardware-and-zinc-coated-track replacement at 5-7 year intervals on every Hawaii residential garage-door installation, tropical hurricane wind exposure driving Hurricane-Iniki-revised tie-down requirements on every Kauai residential garage-door installation, very-high TPCT termite zone 1 pressure across all islands driving pressure-treated wood jamb framing, and Big Island volcanic-ash-fall load review on every Mauna Loa and Kilauea zone-1 lava-flow residential install drives unique tropical-corrosion-and-volcanic-driven material selection.

Hawaii climate, wind & HOA drivers

  • ASCE 7-22 design wind speed: 130 mph (Risk Category II)
  • Hurricane wind tier: High — DASMA 108 wind-pressure rating at 140-150 mph design wind
  • Required garage-door R-value: none (IECC 2021 + state amendments)
  • Dominant residential door material: Steel-insulated panel — dominant in most regions, polyurethane or polystyrene foam core
  • Dominant residential opener type: Belt-drive — dominant in attached-garage residential, quietest operation
  • Termite pressure (TPCT zone): Very high — TPCT zone 1, all wood jamb framing requires PT southern yellow pine .60 CCA
  • HOA architectural-control prevalence: Moderate — suburban HOA prevalence on newer subdivisions
  • Salt-air corrosion zone: High — coastal salt-spray drives stainless-hardware-and-zinc-coated-track requirement at 5-7 year intervals
  • IECC climate zone: 1A

Hawaii licensing & permits

  • License status: Statewide license required
  • License board: Hawaii Department of Commerce & Consumer Affairs — Contractors License Board (C-31 Cement Concrete or C-22b Building Trades / Carpentry) (official site)
  • Permit: county building permit required for new garage-door opening; HI DCCA C-22b license required statewide; mandatory hurricane-rated post-Iniki tie-down detailing on every Kauai residential garage-door installation; volcanic-ash-fall load review on every Big Island Hilo and Kona residential install

How garage door installation costs vary in Hawaii
