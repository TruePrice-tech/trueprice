Roof Replacement Cost in Hawaii (2026)

Roof replacement in Hawaii typically runs $14,500–$38,000 for a 2,000 sq ft asphalt re-roof, with asphalt shingle as the dominant residential covering. Trade-wind exposure on windward (north and east) Oahu and Hawaii Island slopes drives a 130–140 mph design wind; saltwater corrosion on coastal lots requires stainless or hot-dip galvanized fasteners and shortens nominal life of standard-zinc components.

How roof replacement costs vary in Hawaii

Trade-wind exposure on windward (north and east) Oahu and Hawaii Island slopes drives a 130–140 mph design wind; saltwater corrosion on coastal lots requires stainless or hot-dip galvanized fasteners and shortens nominal life of standard-zinc components.

State-specific code or insurance rule: Hawaii's Contractor Recovery Fund (HRS §444-26) caps homeowner restitution from licensed-contractor disputes at $25,000 per claimant — and only licensed C-42b roofers are eligible, making license verification a homeowner-side risk control.
