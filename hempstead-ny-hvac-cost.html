HVAC questions for Hempstead homeowners

What SEER rating should I choose?

16 SEER is the sweet spot for most homeowners. Higher ratings save more on energy but have longer payback periods. In hot climates, 18+ SEER pays back faster.

What size HVAC system do I need?

System size depends on home square footage, insulation quality, window area, and climate zone. A Manual J load calculation determines the correct size. Never accept rule-of-thumb sizing.

How long does a new HVAC system last?

A properly maintained system lasts 15-20 years. Air conditioners and heat pumps last 12-17 years, while gas furnaces can reach 20-25 years with annual maintenance.

How long does HVAC installation take in Hempstead?

A standard residential HVAC replacement takes 1-2 days. Complex jobs involving ductwork modification or system-type changes can take 3-5 days.

How much does a new HVAC system cost in Hempstead?

Most homeowners in Hempstead pay between $4,000 and $14,000 for a new HVAC system, depending on equipment type, efficiency rating, and home size.
