High Point window replacement: answers to common questions

What is argon gas fill in windows?

Argon is an inert gas sealed between panes that insulates better than air. It improves R-value by 15-20% and is standard on most quality double and triple-pane windows. It slowly leaks over 15-20 years.

Should I replace all windows at once?

Replacing all windows at once saves 10-15% on labor versus doing them individually. It also ensures uniform appearance and consistent energy performance throughout the home.

Do replacement windows increase home value?

New windows recover 65-75% of their cost at resale. They also improve curb appeal, reduce noise, and eliminate a common inspection concern for buyers.

What type of replacement window is best?

Vinyl frames offer the best value with low maintenance and good insulation. Fiberglass is more durable and paintable. Wood has the best aesthetics but needs ongoing maintenance. Aluminum is cheapest but conducts heat.
