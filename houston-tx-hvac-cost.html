The A2L rollout is reshaping Houston installs. Every 2026 residential AC or heat pump going into a Houston home runs R-454B (Honeywell's Solstice N41) or R-32 (the Daikin and Mitsubishi preference) under the AIM Act phase-down. HVAC Red Flags and Scams in Houston
