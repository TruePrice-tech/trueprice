Roof Replacement Cost in Illinois (2026)

Roof replacement in Illinois typically runs $9,200–$24,500 for a 2,000 sq ft asphalt re-roof, with asphalt shingle as the dominant residential covering. How roof replacement costs vary in Illinois

The freeze-thaw cycle through Cook, DuPage, and Lake counties (over 70 transitions per winter) drives ice-dam formation and demands continuous ice-and-water shield from eave to 24 inches inside the heated wall, a code-mandatory item under the IRC adoption.

State-specific code or insurance rule: Illinois Roofing Industry Licensing Act (225 ILCS 335) requires every roofing company to hold a single state license tied to a qualifying party — the highest hourly roofer wage in the country (BLS OEWS) reflects the strong union (Local 11) presence in Chicago metro.
