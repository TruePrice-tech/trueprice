How roofing costs vary in Illinois

Roofing prices in Illinois can vary based on labor rates, disposal costs, permit requirements, storm exposure, material availability, and local demand. Larger metro areas may run higher than smaller cities, while coastal or storm prone markets may carry different installation requirements.
