Woogoro — logo concept preview

Each of the 10 ideas from Logo ideas.png, extracted to its own transparent PNG and rendered at realistic sizes: hero (light + dark), in-header lockup at real site chrome, and favicon-size swatches on four backgrounds. Silhouettes invert to white on dark backgrounds via CSS filter: invert(1).

Quick take — starting point for picking

  • Header-friendly (read at 32–36px tall): #1, #2, #5. No frame means the lockup scales down without visual mud.
  • Most distinctive / ownable: #6 (doorway/shire) — ties directly into the Woogoro-shire mythology. Best social-card material.
  • Most "trust" signals: #3 (laurels) and #9 (shield + ribbon). Read as "certified / verified."
  • Frames that fight at small sizes: #7 (curved text in circle), #10 (ornate Victorian frame). Keep these for hero / print only.
  • Hybrid proposal: use #1 or #2 as the header lockup (scales), and #6 or #9 as the hero / social-card / favicon seal (distinctive).
