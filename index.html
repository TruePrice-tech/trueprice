How to Compare Roofing Quotes

Check price per square

Roofing contractors often quote by the square, which equals 100 square feet of roof area.

Compare scope, not just price

A lower quote may exclude flashing, ventilation, or disposal and can become more expensive later.

Review warranty value

Two similar prices can have very different value depending on workmanship and manufacturer warranty.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a roofing square?

A roofing square equals 100 square feet of roof area.

How do I know if a roofing quote is too high?

Compare it against a fair range based on roof size, materials, and job complexity, then compare competing bids side by side.

Should I pick the cheapest roofer?

Not automatically. Very low quotes can mean weaker materials, missing scope, or future change orders.
