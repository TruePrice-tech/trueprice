Frequently Asked Questions

How does TruePrice work?

Three ways to use it: get an estimate before you buy, compare 2-3 quotes side by side, or analyze a single contractor quote you already have. TruePrice uses AI to extract prices, materials, and scope items, then compares against local pricing benchmarks for your city and tells you if the price is fair, what's missing, and what to ask your contractor. No email, no phone, no lead-selling.

What services does TruePrice cover?

16 home services (roofing, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, windows, siding, insulation, painting, fencing, concrete, landscaping, garage doors, solar, foundation repair, kitchen remodeling, and gutters), auto repair, and medical bills. Each has a dedicated analyzer. Home services also include city-specific pricing guides across 14,000+ U.S. cities.

Is TruePrice free?

Yes. The estimate tool, quote analyzer, comparison tool, and all pricing guides are completely free with no signup required.

What does the scope check do?

Every quote is checked against a 12-point scope checklist covering items like tear-off, underlayment, flashing, ventilation, and disposal. Missing items often turn into surprise change orders. TruePrice also scans the fine print for red flags like high deposits, same-day signing pressure, and liability disclaimers.

Can I compare multiple quotes?

Yes. Upload up to 3 quotes and TruePrice compares them side by side on price, scope completeness, warranty, and transparency. It scores each quote, highlights the best value, and generates specific questions to ask each contractor before you sign.

Do you sell my data?

No. Your quote stays in your browser. We don't require an account, email, or phone number. We don't sell leads to contractors. TruePrice is built to help homeowners, not monetize them.
