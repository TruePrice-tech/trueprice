Frequently Asked Questions

How does Woogoro work?

Three ways to use it: get an instant estimate by entering your project details, upload a contractor quote (PDF or image) to analyze it, or compare up to 3 quotes side by side. Woogoro uses AI to extract prices, materials, and scope items, then compares against local pricing benchmarks for your city and tells you if the price is fair, what's missing, and what to ask your contractor.

What services does Woogoro cover?

20 trades total. 19 home services (roofing, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, windows, siding, insulation, painting, fencing, concrete, landscaping, garage doors, solar, foundation repair, kitchen remodeling, gutters, auto repair, moving, and medical bills) plus legal fees. Each has a dedicated estimate page, quote analyzer, and side-by-side comparison tool. City-specific pricing across 12,000+ U.S. cities.

Is Woogoro free?

Yes. The estimate calculator, quote analyzer, comparison tool, and all pricing guides are completely free with no signup required.

What does the scope check do?

Every quote is checked against a 12-point scope checklist covering items like tear-off, underlayment, flashing, ventilation, and disposal. Missing items often turn into surprise change orders. Woogoro also scans the fine print for red flags like high deposits, same-day signing pressure, and liability disclaimers.

Can I compare multiple quotes?

Yes. Upload up to 3 quotes and Woogoro compares them side by side on price, scope completeness, warranty, and transparency. It scores each quote, highlights the best value, and generates specific questions to ask each contractor before you sign.

Do you sell my data?

No. Your quote stays in your browser. We don't require an account, email, or phone number. We don't sell leads to contractors. Woogoro is built to help homeowners, not monetize them.
