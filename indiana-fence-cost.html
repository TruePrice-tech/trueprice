Fence Installation Cost in Indiana (2026)

Installing 150 linear feet of fencing in Indiana typically runs $4,400–$11,500, with 4-to-6-foot galvanized chain-link the dominant residential choice. Concrete-set posts must clear the 32-inch design frost line. Indiana's 32-inch frost line drives mandatory deep-set concrete-anchored posts on every residential fence installation — and the Wabash and White River valley flood-elevation overlay drives below-grade post-footing flood-protection on every full envelope retrofit in FEMA-mapped Tippecanoe and Vanderburgh county properties, with concrete-encased steel or treated wood posts replacing standard wood-post installations on flood-prone parcels.

Indiana climate & post-design drivers

  • Frost-line trench depth: 32-inch design minimum for post footings
  • Dominant residential fence material: Galvanized chain-link — dominant in rural and utility-yard applications
  • Hurricane wind tier: Moderate — 110–140 mph design wind drives 8-foot post spacing
  • Termite pressure (TPCT zone): Moderate — periodic termite pressure
  • HOA fencing-rule prevalence: Moderate — suburban HOA prevalence on newer subdivisions

Indiana licensing, HOA & boundary law

  • License status: No statewide license — municipal credentials only
  • License board: No statewide fencing license — Indianapolis, Fort Wayne, Evansville issue local credentials (official site)
  • Permit: city permit typical for residential fence over 6 feet

How fence installation costs vary in Indiana

State-specific code or insurance rule: Indiana Code §32-26-3 (the Indiana Partition Fence Act) is one of the most detailed boundary-fence statutes in the country — making adjoining landowners equally responsible for half the cost of constructing and maintaining a 'sufficient' partition fence (defined by precise dimensions: 4 feet 6 inches high, posts not more than 12 feet apart for woven-wire, with specific board, rail, or wire materials) — and authorizing township trustees to act as binding arbitrators when neighbors cannot agree.
