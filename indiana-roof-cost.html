Roof Replacement Cost in Indiana (2026)

Roof replacement in Indiana typically runs $7,500–$19,000 for a 2,000 sq ft asphalt re-roof, with asphalt shingle as the dominant residential covering. Tornado-belt activity in the southern half of the state (Evansville, Bloomington, Indianapolis south) drives demand for sealed-edge fastening patterns and 6-nail high-wind shingle warranties, which are upcharges on standard installations.

How roof replacement costs vary in Indiana

Tornado-belt activity in the southern half of the state (Evansville, Bloomington, Indianapolis south) drives demand for sealed-edge fastening patterns and 6-nail high-wind shingle warranties, which are upcharges on standard installations.

State-specific code or insurance rule: Indiana Code §24-5-11 (Home Improvement Contracts Act) voids any contract over $150 that lacks specific written terms — and gives consumers a three-day cancellation window on door-to-door storm-chaser pitches.
