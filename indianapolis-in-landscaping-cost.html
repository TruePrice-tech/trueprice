Irrigation rules and rebates in Indianapolis

Citizens Water of Indianapolis does not mandate fixed residential watering schedules, but the very hard water at 250-350 ppm TDS creates a unique irrigation challenge: mineral buildup clogs drip emitters, coats spray heads with calcium scale, and leaves white residue on ornamental foliage. The dominant Indianapolis landscaping scam is the post-storm or post-tornado door-to-door crew offering tree cleanup and fence repair for $600-$2,500 cash, demanding upfront payment, and disappearing. Indiana does not license general landscape contractors; ISA arborist certification and insurance verification ($1M general liability) are the baseline checks.
