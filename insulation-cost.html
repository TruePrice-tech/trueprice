What Should an Insulation Quote Include?

  • Air sealing
  • Vapor barrier installation
  • Ventilation baffles
  • Removal of old insulation (if needed)
  • Access creation (attic hatch, etc.)
  • Energy audit
  • Permits (if required)
  • Cleanup and disposal
  • Warranty
  • R-value certification
