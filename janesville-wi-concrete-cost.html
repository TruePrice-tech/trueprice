Janesville concrete: answers to common questions

Can cracked concrete be repaired?

Hairline cracks can be sealed with flexible caulk or epoxy for $1-$5 per linear foot. Wider cracks may need patching compound. If the slab has heaved or settled significantly, mudjacking ($5-$10/sqft) or replacement is needed.

How long does concrete last?

Properly poured and maintained concrete lasts 30-50 years. Driveways see 25-30 years with normal use. Sealing every 2-3 years and controlling drainage significantly extend the lifespan.

How much does a concrete patio cost?

A 300 sqft concrete patio costs $1,800-$3,600 for basic broom finish. Stamped patterns add $3-$8/sqft. Colored concrete adds $1-$3/sqft. An exposed aggregate finish adds $2-$4/sqft.

How much does a concrete driveway cost?

A standard 600 sqft concrete driveway costs $3,600-$7,200 for plain finished concrete. Stamped or colored concrete adds $3-$10/sqft. Removing the old driveway adds $1,000-$2,000.

Why pricing varies in Janesville. Janesville's site and climate factors. Janesville has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Air-entrained concrete mix (5-7% air content) is mandatory in Janesville for freeze-thaw resistance. Standard mixes without air entrainment scale and spall within 2-3 winters. Road salt tracking from driveways accelerates surface degradation — apply concrete sealer annually to driveways and walkways exposed to de-icing chemicals. Standard concrete in Janesville runs $6-10 per square foot for basic flatwork (driveways, walkways). Decorative options (stamped, colored, exposed aggregate) add $4-8 per square foot. The biggest hidden cost is demolition and removal of existing concrete — budget $2-4 per square foot for tearout of old slabs.

Janesville paperwork, inspections, and licensing. Most concrete work projects in Janesville require a permit; confirm with the local building department before signing.

Who's doing the work in Janesville. With 63,820 people, Janesville's contractor pool is mid-sized. Lead times are manageable and pricing competition is healthy.
