What kitchen layout is most efficient?

The work triangle (sink, stove, fridge within 4-9 feet of each other) remains the gold standard. L-shaped and U-shaped layouts are most efficient. Galley kitchens work well for single-cook households.

Do I need a permit for a kitchen remodel in Joliet?

In Joliet, permits are required if you move plumbing, add electrical circuits, or change the structural layout. Cosmetic updates like paint, hardware, and countertop swaps do not need permits.
