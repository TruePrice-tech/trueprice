Kansas City Auto Repair Labor Rates

Independent shops in KC charge $75-$125/hour, with the Country Club Plaza, Brookside, and Leawood at the upper end and the East Side, Independence, and Grandview at the lower end. Dealer service departments bill $135-$260/hour. KC pricing is among the most affordable in any major metro.

Kansas City Vehicle Inspections and Warranty Protections

Missouri requires a biennial safety inspection ($12) and a biennial emissions test in the Kansas City area for most vehicles. Kansas does not require emissions testing.
