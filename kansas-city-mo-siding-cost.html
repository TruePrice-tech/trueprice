What red flags should I watch for hiring a siding contractor in Kansas City?

Any Kansas City contractor who asks for more than 30% upfront before materials are ordered is a red flag. Standard practice is 10-15% deposit, materials-on-delivery payment, and final payment on completion. Watch for siding replacement quotes in Kansas City that lack line-item detail. A professional estimate breaks out labor, materials, permits, and cleanup separately. Lump-sum bids hide margin and make change orders impossible to evaluate.
