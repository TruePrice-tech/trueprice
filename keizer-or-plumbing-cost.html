Plumbing in Keizer: what locals should know

💧

Water quality

Keizer benefits from relatively soft water, which is easy on plumbing fixtures and water heaters. You'll get longer appliance life and fewer mineral deposits than homeowners in hard-water areas.

❄

Freeze risk

While Keizer doesn't see extreme cold regularly, freezing temps do occur. Insulate outdoor faucets and any pipes in unheated spaces. Disconnect garden hoses before the first frost.

🔧

Material recommendation

For repiping in Keizer, PEX is the value pick and copper is the premium choice. Both work well in your climate. PEX installs faster (lower labor cost), while copper has a longer track record.

💡

Local tip

In Keizer, homes from the 1970s-80s era are hitting the age where original plumbing starts failing. Water heaters last 8-12 years, and supply pipes 40-60 years. If you're in that window, budget for upgrades.
