HVAC in Kennewick: what locals should know

🌡

Climate and your HVAC

Kennewick's maritime climate means moderate heating needs and minimal AC usage. A ductless mini-split or heat pump system is often the most efficient option.

⚙

System recommendation

In Kennewick, you may not need a gas furnace at all. A heat pump handles the mild winters efficiently, and a supplemental electric strip heater covers the rare deep-freeze event.

📅

Best time to buy

In Kennewick, HVAC demand is more spread out than in extreme climates. Still, fall is the best time for heat pump installations before the wet, cool winter season arrives.

💡

Local tip

Kennewick's mild climate makes this one of the best markets for heat pumps in the country. Many homeowners are switching from gas furnaces and seeing 30-40% energy savings.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new HVAC system cost in Kennewick?

Most homeowners in Kennewick pay between $10,030 and $28,750 for HVAC replacement, depending on system type and efficiency.

Should I replace my AC and furnace at the same time?

Replacing both at once is usually more cost-effective than separate replacements. Matched systems run more efficiently and most contractors offer bundle pricing.

What SEER rating should I choose?

14 SEER is the minimum federal requirement. 16 SEER offers a good balance of efficiency and cost. 18-20+ SEER provides the best energy savings but costs significantly more upfront.
