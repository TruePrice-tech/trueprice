What paint type and prep approach works best in Kirkland?

Constant moisture in Kirkland makes mildew the dominant paint failure mode. Specify 100% acrylic with mildewcide; oil-based paints fail within 3-4 years. Salt air in coastal areas adds corrosion on metal trim and flashing that requires marine-grade primers.
