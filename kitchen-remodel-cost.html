Kitchen Remodel Cost Guide (2026)

By Geoff Lane, founder of Woogoro · Updated April 2026

Compare kitchen remodel costs by scope, size, and city across 1,000+ U.S. locations.

Kitchen remodel costs $19,000 to $75,000+ for most projects. Minor remodels (cabinet refacing, countertops, backsplash) average $19,000 to $30,000. Mid-range full remodels run $30,000 to $60,000. Upscale remodels with custom cabinets exceed $75,000. Labor is 30% to 40% of the budget.

What Should a Kitchen Remodel Quote Include?

  • Demolition and removal
  • Cabinets (stock, semi-custom, or custom)
  • Countertops (granite, quartz, laminate)
  • Flooring
  • Backsplash tile
  • Plumbing (sink, faucet, disposal)
  • Electrical (outlets, lighting circuits)
  • Appliances
  • Lighting (recessed, under-cabinet, pendants)
  • Painting and trim
  • Permits, design, and warranty
