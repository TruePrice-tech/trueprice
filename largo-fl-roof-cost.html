Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new roof cost in Largo?

Most homeowners in Largo pay between $5,800 and $57,250 for a complete roof replacement depending on roof size and materials.

Why do roofing quotes vary so much?

Roofing estimates vary depending on labor assumptions, material brands, flashing work, ventilation upgrades, and warranty coverage included in the proposal.

Should I always choose the lowest roofing quote?

Not always. Lower bids sometimes exclude items like flashing, ventilation upgrades, or disposal costs that appear later as change orders.

Are there special roofing requirements in Largo for hurricanes?

Yes. Largo is in a hurricane-prone area and local building codes typically require enhanced wind uplift ratings and specific fastening patterns. Your contractor should be familiar with local wind-zone requirements.
