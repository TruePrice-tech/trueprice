Frequently Asked Questions

Should I replace the opener when I get a new garage door?

If your opener is more than 10 years old, replacing it with the door is usually more cost-effective. New openers are quieter, more secure, and include modern safety features.

R-Value for Las Vegas Conditions

R-18 polyurethane is essential in Vegas because garage attic temperatures hit 150F in July, and anything stored in an uninsulated garage (paint, electronics, legal documents) degrades visibly in one summer.

UV-stabilized finishes are mandatory in Vegas: UV index averages 11+ for five months, and standard acrylic finishes chalk within 4-5 years. Fluoropolymer coatings (Kynar 500, Fluoropon) hold color 12-15 years and justify their 25-percent premium on south and west-facing installs.

Nevada does not mandate garage-door insulation statewide but Clark County IECC adoption requires U-factor of 0.35 on attached-garage doors. NV Energy's Home Energy Savings program rebates $50-$100 per insulated door above R-14 through the 2026 program cycle.
