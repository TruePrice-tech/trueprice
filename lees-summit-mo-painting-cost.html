Questions Lees Summit homeowners ask about painting

How much does interior painting cost per room?

A typical room costs $300-$700 for walls and ceiling. A full interior (5-7 rooms) runs $2,500-$6,000. Trim, doors, and closets add $100-$300 per room.

Is spray painting better than brush and roller?

Spraying is faster and gives a smoother finish on large, flat surfaces. Brush and roller are better for detail work and touchups. Many pros spray first, then back-roll for adhesion.

How often should a house be painted?

Wood siding needs repainting every 5-7 years. Stucco and fiber cement last 8-12 years between coats. Vinyl and aluminum siding rarely need painting. Climate, sun exposure, and paint quality affect timing.

What should I look for in a painting quote?

A professional quote specifies: paint brand and sheen, number of coats, prep work included, surface repairs, primer usage, timeline, warranty, and what is excluded (trim, doors, ceilings).

What warranty should I expect?

Expect a minimum 1-year workmanship warranty from the contractor plus manufacturer warranties on materials. Get warranty terms in writing before signing.

Should I get multiple quotes?

Always get at least 3 written quotes. Compare line-item details, not just total price. The lowest quote often omits scope items that appear as change orders later.

Never pay more than 10-15% as a deposit before work begins. Standard payment structure: deposit, progress payment when materials arrive, final payment on completion.

About exterior painting in Lee's Summit, MO

Why pricing varies in Lee's Summit. Labor rates here run roughly 15% above the national average per BLS metro data, which is the single biggest driver of how this city's pricing differs from the national midpoint. For exterior painting specifically, Lee's Summit sits within a few points of the national midpoint on our cost model.

Local conditions. Lee's Summit has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Lee's Summit's mix of humidity, moderate UV, and seasonal temperature swings means exterior paint faces all three major degradation pathways. 100% acrylic latex with mildew-resistant primer is the standard — expect 7-10 year repaint cycles with proper prep. Lee's Summit homes averaging 22 years old typically have 1-3 paint layers in good condition. Prep is simpler — power wash, spot-prime bare spots, and apply 2 coats of finish. This keeps the overall project cost lower than older homes with heavy prep requirements.

Permits, codes, and licensing. Most exterior painting projects in Lee's Summit require a permit; confirm with the local building department before signing.

Contractor market. At 93,184 residents, the local pool is moderate; expect 4-6 active contractors for most standard residential work. Lee's Summit's rapid recent growth has tightened the contractor market — expect longer lead times and less negotiating room than smaller markets.
