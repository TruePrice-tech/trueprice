Frequently asked electrical questions

How much does it cost to add an outlet?

Adding a standard outlet costs $150-$300 if wiring can be run through accessible spaces. If walls need to be opened or a new circuit is required, expect $300-$600 per outlet.

How much does a whole-house generator cost in Livonia?

A whole-house standby generator costs $5,000-$15,000 installed in Livonia, depending on wattage (14kW-24kW), fuel type (natural gas or propane), and the transfer switch configuration.

Do I need a permit for electrical work in Livonia?

Most Livonia jurisdictions require permits for new circuits, panel upgrades, and any work beyond simple fixture swaps. Your electrician should handle permitting and schedule the inspection.

How do I know if my home needs rewiring?
