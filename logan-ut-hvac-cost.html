HVAC in Logan: what locals should know

🌡

Climate and your HVAC

Logan homeowners rely heavily on heating from November through March. Proper furnace sizing matters — an oversized unit cycles too frequently and wastes energy.

⚙

System recommendation

In Logan, a high-efficiency gas furnace (95%+ AFUE) paired with a moderate-SEER AC (14-16) gives you the best value. Heating is your biggest expense, so prioritize furnace efficiency.

📅

Best time to buy

In Logan, late summer and early fall are the best times to replace a furnace — before the heating season rush. Spring is ideal for AC replacements before summer demand picks up.

💡

Local tip

Logan homeowners should consider a programmable or smart thermostat with their new system. Setting back temperatures while away or sleeping can save 10-15% on heating bills.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new HVAC system cost in Logan?

Most homeowners in Logan pay between $9,138 and $26,162 for HVAC replacement, depending on system type and efficiency.

Should I replace my AC and furnace at the same time?

Replacing both at once is usually more cost-effective than separate replacements. Matched systems run more efficiently and most contractors offer bundle pricing.

What SEER rating should I choose?

14 SEER is the minimum federal requirement. 16 SEER offers a good balance of efficiency and cost. 18-20+ SEER provides the best energy savings but costs significantly more upfront.
