Roof Replacement Cost in Los Angeles CA

The average roof replacement cost in Los Angeles CA typically ranges from $12,000 to $28,000 depending on roof size, roofing material, tear off requirements, pitch, and overall complexity.

For many homeowners, a standard architectural shingle roof replacement in Los Angeles falls in the $15,000 to $22,000 range, though tile roofs, complex rooflines, and premium materials can increase total cost significantly.

Typical Roofing Prices in Los Angeles CA

  • Architectural shingles: $650 to $1,000 per square
  • Metal roofing: $1,100 to $2,000 per square
  • Clay or concrete tile roofing: $1,500 to $3,500 per square

Example Roof Replacement Pricing in Los Angeles

A typical 2,200 sq ft home in Los Angeles may see quotes in roughly this range:

  • Low estimate: $15,000
  • Fair range: $18,000 to $23,000
  • High estimate: $30,000+

What Affects Roof Replacement Cost in Los Angeles CA?

  • Roof size and number of stories
  • Roof pitch and jobsite complexity
  • Material selection (tile roofs are very common in Southern California)
  • Local labor costs and permit requirements
  • Tear off and disposal needs
  • Fire-resistant roofing requirements and building codes

How to Compare Roofing Quotes in Los Angeles CA

Do not compare roofing quotes by price alone. A lower quote may exclude flashing replacement, ventilation upgrades, underlayment quality, tear off, disposal, or warranty coverage.

The best way to compare quotes is to review total scope, roofing materials, workmanship warranty, manufacturer warranty, cleanup, and clearly stated exclusions.
