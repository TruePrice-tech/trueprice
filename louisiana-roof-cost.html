Roof Replacement Cost in Louisiana (2026)

Roof replacement in Louisiana typically runs $8,500–$23,000 for a 2,000 sq ft asphalt re-roof, with asphalt shingle as the dominant residential covering. ASCE 7-22 wind speeds along Cameron, Plaquemines, and St. Bernard parishes reach 150–160 mph for design wind — Miami-Dade-style enhanced fastening (6 nails, 1.25-inch ring shank) and starter-strip drip-edge are standard.

How roof replacement costs vary in Louisiana

ASCE 7-22 wind speeds along Cameron, Plaquemines, and St. Bernard parishes reach 150–160 mph for design wind — Miami-Dade-style enhanced fastening (6 nails, 1.25-inch ring shank) and starter-strip drip-edge are standard.

State-specific code or insurance rule: Louisiana Revised Statute §22:1264 enacted post-Katrina caps the deductible-rebating prohibition with $5,000 fines per violation; the Louisiana Citizens Property Insurance Corporation provides last-resort hurricane coverage for coastal homeowners denied private market policies.
