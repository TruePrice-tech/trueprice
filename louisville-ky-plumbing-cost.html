Plumbing in Louisville, KY: what locals should know

Climate & site factors

Louisville has moderately hard water which can reduce water heater lifespan over time. Consider a water softener or tankless unit with a descaling system to protect your investment. For Louisville's hard water, PEX piping is ideal — it doesn't corrode or develop pinhole leaks like copper can in mineral-rich water. It's also the most cost-effective option for whole-house repiping.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a plumber cost in Louisville?

Plumber rates in Louisville typically range from $85-$150/hour. Total project costs depend on the scope — a simple drain cleaning may cost $200-$450 while a water heater replacement runs $1,500-$4,500.

Should I repair or replace my water heater?

If your water heater is over 10 years old or the repair cost exceeds 50% of replacement, replacing is usually the better investment. Tankless water heaters last 20+ years and can reduce energy costs.

Is PEX or copper better for repiping?

PEX is 30-40% cheaper than copper and faster to install. Copper lasts longer (50+ years vs 25-30 for PEX) and has higher resale value. Both are code-compliant in most areas.
