How does Macon's humidity affect electrical service strategy choice?

High humidity in Macon accelerates corrosion on outdoor electrical connections and weatherheads. Outdoor GFCI outlets and panel enclosures degrade faster here than in dry climates, adding maintenance costs that contractors factor into their quotes.

What signs of a bad electrician should Macon homeowners watch for?

Be cautious of electrical work contractors in Macon who pressure you to sign same-day. Legitimate contractors expect you to get competing bids and will hold their price for 30 days. High-pressure sales tactics correlate with inflated pricing. Any Macon contractor who asks for more than 30% upfront before materials are ordered is a red flag.
