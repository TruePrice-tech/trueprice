Frequently asked foundation repair questions

What are the signs of foundation problems?

Warning signs include diagonal cracks above door frames, sticking doors and windows, uneven or sloping floors, gaps between walls and ceiling, and cracks wider than 1/4 inch in the foundation wall.

How long does foundation repair take?

Most pier installations take 1-3 days. Interior waterproofing takes 2-5 days. Major structural repair can take 1-2 weeks. Minimal disruption to daily life during exterior pier work; interior work is more invasive.

How much does foundation repair cost in Madison?

Foundation repair in Madison typically costs $2,000-$7,000 for minor crack sealing, $5,000-$15,000 for pier installation, and $15,000-$30,000+ for major structural repair or underpinning.

What is the difference between push piers and helical piers?

Push piers are driven to bedrock or stable soil using the structure's weight. Helical piers are screwed in mechanically. Push piers work best for heavy structures. Helical piers work in lighter structures and new construction.

What Madison's local context means for pricing. Madison has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Madison's freeze-thaw cycles affect foundations differently than warm climates. Frost heave pushes shallow foundations upward, and ice lens formation in clay soils creates lateral pressure on basement walls. Proper drainage (perimeter drains, sump pumps) and insulation below grade are the standard preventive measures.
