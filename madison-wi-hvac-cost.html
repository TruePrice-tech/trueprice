HVAC in Madison: what locals should know

🌡

Climate and your HVAC

In Madison, winters are long and brutal — your heating system is your most critical home appliance. Don't skimp on furnace quality; breakdowns in sub-zero weather are dangerous.

⚙

System recommendation

In Madison, prioritize furnace reliability and BTU capacity. Undersizing is risky in extreme cold. Get a proper Manual J load calculation — don't let contractors just match the old unit's size.

📅

Best time to buy

Don't gamble on an aging furnace in Madison's winters. If your system is 15+ years old, schedule a replacement in early fall when you can plan rather than panic.

💡

Local tip

In Madison, a furnace failure in January can be dangerous. Keep an emergency plan: know a reliable HVAC company, consider a backup heat source, and don't ignore warning signs like strange noises or uneven heating.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new HVAC system cost in Madison?

Most homeowners in Madison pay between $8,075 and $23,173 for HVAC replacement, depending on system type and efficiency.

Should I replace my AC and furnace at the same time?

Replacing both at once is usually more cost-effective than separate replacements. Matched systems run more efficiently and most contractors offer bundle pricing.

What SEER rating should I choose?

14 SEER is the minimum federal requirement. 16 SEER offers a good balance of efficiency and cost. 18-20+ SEER provides the best energy savings but costs significantly more upfront.
