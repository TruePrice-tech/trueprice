Memphis Efficiency Targets: SEER and AFUE

Cooling efficiency target

16+ SEER

16 SEER is the sensible Memphis minimum because heating, not cooling, dominates your Memphis Light, Gas bill. The 14-to-16 SEER jump delivers modest savings here; the 16-to-20 jump delivers almost none. Put the efficiency budget toward the furnace or a cold-climate heat pump instead.

Heating efficiency target

90%+ AFUE

Memphis's heating season is meaningful but not extreme, so 90-92% AFUE is the sensible sweet spot. If you're replacing an 80% AFUE unit in an older neighborhood home, the jump to 92% saves $200-$400 per year at Memphis Light, Gas's current gas rate; the marginal step to 96% takes meaningfully longer to pay back.
