Roofing in Memphis: what locals should know

☁️

Weather & climate

Memphis faces moderate hail risk from spring supercells, extreme summer heat and humidity, and occasional ice storms that can damage older roofing systems

🧱

Best materials for Memphis

Algae-resistant architectural shingles offer the best value in Memphis's mixed humid climate, balancing heat resistance with durability through seasonal temperature swings

📅

Local market

The moderate pace of construction in Memphis means homeowners can often find available roofers with 1-2 weeks notice outside storm season

📜

Permits

The Memphis Building Inspections office requires a permit for full roof replacements to ensure compliance with local building codes

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new roof cost in Memphis?

Most homeowners in Memphis pay between $6,450 and $63,950 for a complete roof replacement depending on roof size and materials.

Why do roofing quotes vary so much?

Roofing estimates vary depending on labor assumptions, material brands, flashing work, ventilation upgrades, and warranty coverage included in the proposal.

Should I always choose the lowest roofing quote?

Not always. Lower bids sometimes exclude items like flashing, ventilation upgrades, or disposal costs that appear later as change orders.

My home in Memphis is older. Does that affect the cost?

Often yes. Homes in Memphis average around 37 years old. Older roofs may need additional decking repair, updated ventilation, or code-required upgrades that add to the base replacement cost.
