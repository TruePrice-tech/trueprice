Architectural Shingle Roof Cost

Architectural shingles are the most common residential roofing material in North America. They provide a balance of durability, appearance, and cost, making them a popular choice for homeowners replacing an aging roof.

Average Architectural Shingle Roof Cost

Roof Size Typical Cost Range
1,500 sq ft roof $7,000 to $12,000
2,000 sq ft roof $9,000 to $15,000
2,500 sq ft roof $11,000 to $18,000
3,000 sq ft roof $13,000 to $22,000

Cost Per Square Foot

Architectural shingle roofs typically cost between $4 and $8 per square foot installed. Pricing varies based on roof complexity, labor conditions, and material quality.

Homes with steep roofs, multiple valleys, skylights, or dormers may see higher installation costs because of increased labor requirements.

What Affects Shingle Roof Pricing

  • Roof size and complexity
  • Number of valleys and roof penetrations
  • Underlayment quality
  • Flashing replacement scope
  • Ventilation upgrades
  • Tear off and disposal
