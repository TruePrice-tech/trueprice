Metal Roof Replacement Cost (2026)

Metal roofs have become increasingly popular because they can last 40–70 years and offer excellent weather resistance. However they cost more upfront than asphalt shingles.

Average Metal Roof Cost

Most homeowners pay between:

  • $8 – $16 per square foot installed
  • $16,000 – $45,000 total project cost

Actual pricing depends on roof size, material type, roof complexity, labor, and flashing requirements.

Metal Roof Cost by Roof Size

Roof Size Estimated Cost

Standing Seam vs Metal Panels

Roof Type Typical Cost
Corrugated Metal Panels $8 – $12 / sq ft
Standing Seam Metal $12 – $16 / sq ft

What Drives Metal Roof Pricing

  • Standing seam vs exposed fastener panels
  • Roof pitch and complexity
  • Underlayment upgrades
  • Flashing and trim details
  • Labor costs
