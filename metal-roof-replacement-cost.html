Metal Roof Replacement Cost

Metal roofs are popular because they can last much longer than asphalt shingles and perform well in severe weather. They also cost more upfront, so it is important to compare price, system type, and quote details carefully.

Average Metal Roof Cost

Most homeowners pay between:

  • $8 to $16 per square foot installed
  • $16,000 to $45,000 total project cost

Actual pricing depends on roof size, material type, roof complexity, labor, trim details, and flashing scope.

Metal Roof Cost by Roof Size

Roof Size Estimated Cost

Standing Seam vs Metal Panels

Roof Type Typical Cost
Corrugated metal panels $8 to $12 per sq ft
Standing seam metal $12 to $16 per sq ft

What Drives Metal Roof Pricing

  • Standing seam vs exposed fastener panels
  • Roof pitch and complexity
  • Underlayment upgrades
  • Flashing and trim details
  • Labor costs
