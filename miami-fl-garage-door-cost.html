Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new garage door cost in Miami?

Most homeowners in Miami pay between $999 and $4,140 for a new garage door installed, depending on size and material.

Should I replace the opener when I get a new garage door?

If your opener is more than 10 years old, replacing it with the door is usually more cost-effective. New openers are quieter, more secure, and include modern safety features.

How long does garage door installation take?

A standard garage door installation takes 3-5 hours. Custom doors or jobs requiring structural modifications may take a full day.

About garage door replacement in Miami, FL

Why pricing varies in Miami. Labor rates here track within a few points of the national average, so most of the price variation comes from material costs and local market dynamics rather than wage differentials. For garage door replacement specifically, Miami sits within a few points of the national midpoint on our cost model.

Local conditions. Miami sits in one of the nation's highest hurricane risk zones, with the Atlantic hurricane season from June through November driving strict roofing code requirements Miami sees moderate hail risk, hurricane exposure — all of which contractors here have to plan for. Specify wind-rated doors (DASMA 110 minimum, 130+ in coastal counties) — most insurance carriers now require it.

Permits, codes, and licensing. Most garage door replacement projects in Miami require a permit; confirm with the local building department before signing.

Contractor market. With a population of 417,650, the local market has enough contractors to drive real competition on price, but you should still get three quotes. Miami has seen steady growth, which means a healthy supply of established contractors with multi-year track records.
