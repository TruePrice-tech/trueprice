Skipping the permit in Miami voids Rheem's warranty and any other manufacturer coverage, creates an MLS-listed defect at sale time, and sticks you with liability: a botched Miami AC install leaks refrigerant into the attic, drips condensate into the Florida Power & Light (FPL)-side electrical panel, or fails its first pressure test during July; Miami-Dade County Building Department catches those upstream of drywall closing back up.
