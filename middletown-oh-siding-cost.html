Frequently asked siding questions

How much does siding replacement cost in Middletown?

Siding replacement in Middletown costs $6,000-$18,000 for an average home. Vinyl is the most affordable ($4-$8/sqft installed), while fiber cement ($8-$14/sqft) and wood ($10-$18/sqft) cost more.

What is the most energy-efficient siding?

Insulated vinyl siding (with foam backing) adds R-2 to R-5 insulation. Fiber cement over rigid foam sheathing provides better thermal performance. The biggest energy gains come from fixing air leaks during installation.

What goes under siding?

From inside out: wall sheathing (plywood or OSB), a weather-resistant barrier (house wrap like Tyvek), then siding. Some installations add rigid foam insulation between the house wrap and siding.
