Landscape Installation Cost in Mississippi (2026)

Landscape installation in Mississippi typically runs $3,600–$15,600 for a front-and-back-yard residential landscape install, with cool- or warm-season turf-dominant front-yard design as the dominant residential approach across USDA hardiness zone 7b-9a. Mississippi's combination of warm-humid Gulf Coast climate from Bay St. Louis to Pascagoula, alluvial Mississippi River Delta soils across the Western Mississippi counties from Greenville to Vicksburg, and 240-day growing season supports near-year-round residential landscape demand on warm-season Bermuda, zoysia, and centipede turf — and the Mississippi pine-belt across the Southern Pine Hills from Hattiesburg to Laurel supports loblolly and longleaf pine canopy that drives fungicide-rotation programs for take-all root rot on every St. Augustinegrass install in the coastal corridor.

Mississippi climate, water & plant-palette drivers

  • USDA hardiness zone: 7b-9a — drives plant-palette selection and frost-survival ratings
  • Annual rainfall: 56 inches/yr — drives irrigation system sizing
  • Growing season: 240 frost-free days per year
  • Drought tier: Moderate — periodic drought, advisory water-saving guidelines
  • Irrigation dependency: Moderate — periodic irrigation needed during dry season
  • Frost-line trench depth: 6-inch design minimum for irrigation backflow + bed prep
  • Dominant residential aesthetic: Turf-dominant — cool- or warm-season residential lawn as primary front-yard ground cover
  • Wildfire defensible-space rule: No WUI defensible-space requirement

Mississippi licensing, pesticide & permits

  • License status: Statewide license required
  • License board: Mississippi Department of Agriculture & Commerce Bureau of Plant Industry — Pesticide Section + Mississippi State Board of Contractors (official site)
  • Permit: no permit required for landscape install; MDAC Commercial Pesticide Applicator required for any chemical lawn care; MSBC residential license required above $50,000 contract
