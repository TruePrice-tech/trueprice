Mobile gutter FAQ

How much do new gutters cost in Mobile?

Gutters in Mobile cost $6-$15 per linear foot for aluminum seamless, $8-$20 for steel, and $15-$30 for copper. A typical home (150-200 linear feet) runs $1,200-$3,000 for aluminum.

What causes gutters to pull away from the house?

Common causes: rotted fascia board behind the gutter, failed or spaced-too-far-apart hangers, ice and snow weight, clogged gutters holding heavy water, and improper original installation.

Do gutters protect the foundation?

Yes. Gutters prevent concentrated roof runoff from eroding soil next to the foundation and saturating the ground. Homes without gutters have 3-5x more foundation moisture problems than homes with properly functioning gutter systems.

What drives gutter installation cost in Mobile. BLS data shows Mobile's trade labor rates at about 11% below the US median. Site-specific factors around Mobile. Mobile has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Gutters in Mobile must handle heavy summer downpours and constant debris from vigorous plant growth. Oversize to 6-inch K-style minimum and add kickout flashing where gutters meet siding — undersized 5-inch gutters overflow during the intense rain events common here. Leaf guards are essential with the year-round vegetation. Seamless aluminum gutters are the Mobile standard: $6-12 per linear foot installed. Copper ($20-40/ft) and zinc ($15-25/ft) offer 50+ year lifespans but at 3-4x the cost. For most homeowners, aluminum with a quality finish provides the best value at 20-30 years before replacement.
