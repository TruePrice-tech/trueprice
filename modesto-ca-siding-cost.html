Frequently Asked Questions

How much does new siding cost in Modesto?

Most homeowners in Modesto pay between $6,750 and $23,650 for siding replacement, depending on material and home size.

Is fiber cement siding worth the extra cost?

Fiber cement (like James Hardie) costs more than vinyl but lasts 30-50 years, resists fire and termites, and adds more resale value. It is a popular mid-range upgrade.

How long does siding replacement take?

Most siding projects take 1-2 weeks depending on home size and material. Vinyl installs fastest, while fiber cement and wood take longer.

About siding replacement in Modesto, CA

Why pricing varies in Modesto. Labor rates here run roughly 20% above the national average per BLS metro data, which is the single biggest driver of how this city's pricing differs from the national midpoint. For siding replacement specifically, our cost-multiplier model puts Modesto at roughly 16% above the national midpoint.

Local conditions. Modesto has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. UV is the dominant siding concern in Modesto. Standard vinyl fades and becomes brittle within 8-10 years under intense sun. Stucco and fiber cement are the local standards for good reason — they handle UV and heat without the color shift and cracking that plague vinyl on south and west exposures. Siding costs in Modesto vary dramatically by material: vinyl ($4-8/sqft installed), fiber cement ($8-14/sqft), engineered wood ($9-15/sqft), cedar ($10-18/sqft), and stone veneer ($15-30/sqft). For a 2,000 sqft exterior, that's $8,000-60,000 — material choice is the single biggest pricing decision.

Permits, codes, and licensing. Most siding replacement projects in Modesto require a permit; confirm with the local building department before signing.
