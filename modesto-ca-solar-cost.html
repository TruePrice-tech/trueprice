What Modesto residents ask about solar

Should I buy or lease solar panels?

Buying maximizes long-term savings and increases home value. Leasing eliminates upfront cost but the leasing company keeps the tax credit, and panels may complicate home sales.

Does Modesto have any state or local solar incentives?

Incentives vary. Check the DSIRE database for Modesto-specific rebates, SRECs, property tax exemptions, and performance-based incentives that stack on top of the federal 30% ITC.

How does the federal solar tax credit work?

The Investment Tax Credit (ITC) lets you deduct 30% of your total solar installation cost from your federal taxes. It applies to purchased systems, not leased. There is no cap on the credit amount.

How many panels do I need?

Panel count depends on your electricity usage, panel wattage, and roof sun exposure. A typical home uses 20-30 panels (400W each) to offset 80-100% of electricity consumption.

How much does solar cost in Modesto?

Most homeowners in Modesto pay $15,000-$30,000 before incentives for a 6-10kW system. After the 30% federal tax credit, net cost drops to $10,500-$21,000.

How much solar energy can I produce in Modesto?

Modesto is one of the best locations in the country for residential solar production. A standard 8kW system produces 12,000-14,000 kWh annually, 25-35% more than average U.S. markets. Most Modesto homeowners can cover 80-100% of their electricity needs with a properly sized system. Plan for future consumption increases from EVs or pool equipment when determining system size.

What solar incentives are available in Modesto?

Start with the 30% federal ITC, which applies to all Modesto solar installations with no cap. Then check DSIRE for CA-level incentives: state tax credits, utility rebates, SREC markets, property tax exemptions, and net metering terms. Each utility has different programs and rates. The combined federal-plus-state incentive stack typically reduces net cost by 40-60% in markets with strong state programs.

When should I install solar in Modesto?

The best installation window in Modesto is fall or winter, cooler temperatures make attic work comfortable and your system starts producing before peak summer rates. Note that the process from quote to installation takes 6-12 weeks including permitting, utility approval, and scheduling. Start the process 2-3 months before your target installation date. The federal tax credit applies to the year of installation, so timing relative to tax year can matter.
