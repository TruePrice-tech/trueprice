What Murray residents ask about garage door

How much does a garage door opener cost?

A new opener installed costs $250-$600 for chain or belt drive. Smart openers with Wi-Fi and battery backup run $400-$800 installed. Replacing the opener alone takes 2-3 hours.

What is the difference between chain and belt drive openers?

Chain drive is cheaper ($150-$250) but louder. Belt drive ($200-$350) is whisper-quiet and ideal when bedrooms are above or adjacent to the garage. Both have similar reliability.

Should I repair or replace my garage door?

Repair if damage is limited to one or two panels and replacements are available. Replace if the door is over 15 years old, panels are no longer manufactured, or you want better insulation and security.

What R-value garage door do I need in Murray?

Murray's winters demand serious insulation. An R-12 to R-16 door is the baseline for attached garages, with R-16 to R-18 recommended if you use the space as a workshop or heat it. The $200-$500 upgrade cost pays back through lower heating bills in rooms adjacent to the garage within 3-5 years.

Should I repair or replace my garage door in Murray?

Given that Murray homes average 39 years old, many original garage doors are due for replacement rather than repair. A new insulated door and modern opener runs $1,200-$3,000 and recoups 90-100% at resale. Repairs make financial sense for doors under 15 years old with specific damage, but throwing money at an aging system usually delays an inevitable replacement.

Best time of year to replace a garage door in Murray?

Murray homeowners get the best pricing and selection by scheduling garage door replacement during late spring or early fall. Moderate temperatures allow proper weatherstrip sealing and caulk curing, and you can save 5-10% versus peak season pricing. Spring demand surges from cold-weather spring failures, so plan ahead to avoid the rush.
