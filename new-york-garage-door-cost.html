Garage Door Replacement Cost in New York (2026)

Garage door replacements in New York typically run $1,500–$5,400 for a 16x7 single-bay or 18x7 double-bay steel-insulated garage door + opener replacement, with steel-insulated panel the dominant residential choice and 130 mph ASCE 7-22 design wind speed driving DASMA wind-pressure rating selection. New York's combination of 48-inch frost depth across the Adirondack region, F3 freeze-thaw cycling across the Adirondacks and Catskills, persistent Atlantic salt-spray on every NYC, Suffolk, Nassau, Westchester, and Long Island coastal exposure driving stainless-hardware-and-zinc-coated-track replacement at 5-7 year intervals, FEMA NFIP base-flood-elevation foundation height verification on every NYC and Long Island coastal residential install post-Sandy, and New York Energy Code Title 19 Part 1240 above-IECC-baseline garage-door R-value mandate drives extreme cold-climate-and-coastal-driven material selection.

New York climate, wind & HOA drivers

  • ASCE 7-22 design wind speed: 130 mph (Risk Category II)
  • Hurricane wind tier: Moderate — DASMA 108 wind-pressure rating at 110-140 mph design wind
  • Required garage-door R-value: R-13 (IECC 2021 + state amendments)
  • Dominant residential door material: Steel-insulated panel — dominant in most regions, polyurethane or polystyrene foam core
  • Dominant residential opener type: Belt-drive — dominant in attached-garage residential, quietest operation
  • Termite pressure (TPCT zone): Low — minimal termite pressure
  • HOA architectural-control prevalence: Moderate — suburban HOA prevalence on newer subdivisions
  • Salt-air corrosion zone: High — coastal salt-spray drives stainless-hardware-and-zinc-coated-track requirement at 5-7 year intervals
  • IECC climate zone: 4A-6A

New York licensing & permits

  • License status: No statewide license — municipal credentials only
  • License board: No statewide garage-door license — NYC Department of Buildings and most NY home-rule municipalities issue Building Contractor licenses (official site)
  • Permit: city or town building permit required for new garage-door opening; NYC DOB Construction Superintendent license required for new-construction; NY Energy Code Title 19 Part 1240 + 2020 Stretch Code mandatory; FEMA NFIP base-flood-elevation foundation height required on every NYC and Long Island coastal residential install post-Sandy

How garage door installation costs vary in New York

State-specific code or insurance rule: New York is one of the few large states without a statewide residential builder license — NYC Department of Buildings, Long Island municipalities, Westchester, Suffolk, and Nassau counties issue local Building Contractor credentials but no centralized state board oversees garage-door contractor competency or bond requirements, plus New York Energy Conservation Construction Code (Title 19 NYCRR Part 1240) separately mandates above-IECC-2021-baseline garage-door R-13 minimum insulation on every NY residential installation, and NYC Local Law 1 separately requires historic-preservation review on every NYC residential garage-door installation within an LPC-mapped historic district.
