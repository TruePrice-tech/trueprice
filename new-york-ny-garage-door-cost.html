Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new garage door cost in New York?

Most homeowners in New York pay between $1,211 and $4,945 for a new garage door installed, depending on size and material.

Should I replace the opener when I get a new garage door?

If your opener is more than 10 years old, replacing it with the door is usually more cost-effective. New openers are quieter, more secure, and include modern safety features.

How long does garage door installation take?

A standard garage door installation takes 3-5 hours. Custom doors or jobs requiring structural modifications may take a full day.

R-Value for New York Conditions

R-16 polyurethane is the practical floor in NYC because Staten Island garages share walls with finished basements and the 2020 NYC Energy Conservation Construction Code enforces the 0.35 U-factor ceiling.

Bottom seals have to survive salt spray from DOT winter plowing, which destroys cheap vinyl within 2 seasons; EPDM rubber seals are the upgrade standard here. Con Edison rebates up to $75 per insulated door above R-14 through the 2026 EmPower program.

New York DOB requires a flame-spread Class A rating on any door sharing a wall with habitable space, which rules out cheap polystyrene-board-only construction. Polyurethane-injected doors meet the Class A requirement automatically through the encapsulating steel skins.

New York Spring and Cable Systems
