Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a paver patio cost in Norwalk?

Most homeowners in Norwalk pay between $7,100 and $14,100 for a paver patio, depending on size and materials chosen.

How long does a paver patio last?

A properly installed paver patio can last 25-50 years with minimal maintenance. Pavers can be individually replaced if damaged, making them a long-lasting investment.

Do I need a permit for landscaping work?

Permits are typically required for retaining walls over 4 feet tall, major grading changes, and drainage work that affects neighboring properties. Check with your local building department in Norwalk.
