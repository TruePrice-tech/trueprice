Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a paver patio cost in Olathe?

A properly installed paver patio can last 25-50 years with minimal maintenance. Pavers can be individually replaced if damaged, making them a long-lasting investment.

Permits are typically required for retaining walls over 4 feet tall, major grading changes, and drainage work that affects neighboring properties. Check with your local building department in Olathe.
