Siding questions for Olathe homeowners

Can new siding go over old siding?

Overlaying is possible but not recommended. It hides potential water damage, adds weight, and can void warranties. Tear-off adds $1,000-$3,000 but lets you inspect and repair sheathing and moisture barriers.

Should I paint or replace my siding?

Paint if siding is structurally sound with no rot or warping. Painting costs $3,000-$7,000 and lasts 5-10 years. If paint peels repeatedly or siding has damage, replacement is the better long-term investment.

Is vinyl or fiber cement siding better?

Fiber cement resists fire, impact, and insects better than vinyl, and holds paint longer. Vinyl costs 30-40% less, never needs painting, and handles moisture well. Choose based on budget and climate.
