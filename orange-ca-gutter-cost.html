Orange gutter FAQ

Should I repair or replace my gutters?

Repair if damage is limited to a few sections, joints, or downspouts. Replace if gutters sag along most of their length, have multiple leaks, are rusting through, or the fascia behind them is rotting.

How much do new gutters cost in Orange?

Gutters in Orange cost $6-$15 per linear foot for aluminum seamless, $8-$20 for steel, and $15-$30 for copper. A typical home (150-200 linear feet) runs $1,200-$3,000 for aluminum.

What causes gutters to pull away from the house?

Common causes: rotted fascia board behind the gutter, failed or spaced-too-far-apart hangers, ice and snow weight, clogged gutters holding heavy water, and improper original installation.

Do gutters protect the foundation?

Yes. Gutters prevent concentrated roof runoff from eroding soil next to the foundation and saturating the ground. Homes without gutters have 3-5x more foundation moisture problems than homes with properly functioning gutter systems.

Are gutter guards worth it?

Gutter guards reduce cleaning frequency by 80-90% but do not eliminate it entirely. Quality guards cost $7-$15 per linear foot installed. They pay back in 5-8 years through reduced cleaning costs and prevented water damage.
