Orem concrete: answers to common questions

How much does concrete work cost in Orem?

Concrete costs in Orem range from $6-$12 per square foot for basic flatwork (driveways, patios) to $15-$30/sqft for stamped or decorative finishes, including labor and materials.

What is the difference between concrete and cement?

Cement is an ingredient in concrete. Concrete is a mix of cement (10-15%), water (15-20%), and aggregates (sand and gravel, 60-75%). Concrete is the finished building material; cement is the binding powder.

How much does a concrete foundation cost?

A full basement foundation costs $15,000-$30,000. A slab foundation for a house costs $5,000-$15,000. A garage slab costs $2,500-$6,000. Price depends on size, depth, and soil conditions.

What Orem homeowners should know about concrete work

Why pricing varies in Orem. Labor rates here track within a few points of the national average, so most of the price variation comes from material costs and local market dynamics rather than wage differentials. Our cost model shows concrete work in Orem tracking right at the national baseline.

Orem's site and climate factors. Orem has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Air-entrained concrete mix (5-7% air content) is mandatory in Orem for freeze-thaw resistance. Standard mixes without air entrainment scale and spall within 2-3 winters. Road salt tracking from driveways accelerates surface degradation — apply concrete sealer annually to driveways and walkways exposed to de-icing chemicals. Homes in Orem averaging 35 years old typically have stable sub-grades that simplify concrete work. New pours integrate cleanly with existing slabs and foundations without the settling corrections older properties need.

Orem paperwork, inspections, and licensing. Most concrete work projects in Orem require a permit; confirm with the local building department before signing.

Who's doing the work in Orem. With 91,648 people, Orem's contractor pool is mid-sized. You will find 4-6 established shops for most trades, which provides enough competition for fair pricing. Orem's measured growth has built a reliable contractor ecosystem. Lead times are manageable and pricing competition is healthy.
