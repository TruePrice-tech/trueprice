Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new garage door cost in Orlando?

Most homeowners in Orlando pay between $935 and $3,795 for a new garage door installed, depending on size and material.

Should I replace the opener when I get a new garage door?

If your opener is more than 10 years old, replacing it with the door is usually more cost-effective. New openers are quieter, more secure, and include modern safety features.

How long does garage door installation take?

A standard garage door installation takes 3-5 hours. Custom doors or jobs requiring structural modifications may take a full day.

Opener Preferences in Orlando

Orlando homeowners commonly install LiftMaster or Chamberlain belt-drive openers with Wi-Fi connectivity and battery backup. Battery backup is essential for hurricane-season power outages, and manual-release mechanisms must be accessible. Chain-drive openers remain popular in detached garages where noise is less of a concern.

Permits, Code, and Dealers in Orlando

City of Orlando Permitting Services Division handles garage door replacement permits when structural headers or opening dimensions change. Florida Building Code requires wind-load-rated doors in the HVHZ and high-wind zones. Miami-Dade NOA or Florida Product Approval is required for compliant products.

Clopay, Amarr, and Wayne Dalton are the major brands distributed through Orlando dealers. Winter Park and Dr. Phillips have strong local dealer networks offering Overhead Door and C.H.I. products. Specialty carriage-house and custom wood doors are available through regional distributors.

Orlando is in a hurricane zone and garage doors are the most vulnerable large opening in the building envelope. Wind-rated doors with reinforcement struts and heavy-duty track are mandatory.

Orlando's climate is characterized by humid subtropical with hurricane exposure, intense UV, year-round humidity, and frequent severe summer thunderstorms, which affects every garage door spec decision discussed above.

Local soil conditions also matter for garage slab integrity: sandy with karst features in some areas (sinkhole-prone). This affects track alignment and long-term door performance. Permit timeline in Orlando: City of Orlando and Orange County both issue residential roof permits within 1-3 days.
