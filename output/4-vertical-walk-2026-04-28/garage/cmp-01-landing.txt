How to compare garage door quotes correctly

Garage door quotes vary by door material, insulation, and spring quality. A builder-grade uninsulated steel door and an insulated composite door are very different products even at similar sizes.

Torsion springs last 2-3x longer than extension springs and are the right choice for any door over 8 feet wide. Opener horsepower and smart connectivity (MyQ, HomeKit) also affect price meaningfully.

Compare track and hardware replacement, old door haul-off, and warranty on door, springs, and opener separately — these are often different warranty terms.
