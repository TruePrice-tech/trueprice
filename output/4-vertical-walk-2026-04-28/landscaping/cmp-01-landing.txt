How to compare landscaping quotes correctly

Landscaping quotes are the widest-ranging in home services because 'landscaping' means everything from a lawn install to a full hardscape. Scope must be defined in plants, square footage, and irrigation zones before you can compare.

Plant sizes matter. A 3-gallon shrub and a 7-gallon shrub of the same species can differ by $40+ each. Sod square footage, soil amendment depth, and mulch depth should all be spelled out.

Compare warranty on plants (typically 1 year if watered properly), irrigation coverage, and drainage work. Hardscape elements like edging, pavers, or retaining walls should be itemized separately.
