Next Steps
Ask what paint brand and product line will be used (premium paints last longer)
Confirm the number of coats included (2 coats is standard for a quality job)
Ask about surface prep: will they scrape, sand, and prime where needed?
Check if the quote includes caulking, trim painting, and cleanup
Ways to Save
Off-season scheduling: painting in late fall or winter can save 10-15%
Bundle interior + exterior in one project for crew-day discounts
Supply your own paint — many contractors mark up 15-30% on paint
Premium paints (Sherwin-Williams Emerald, Benjamin Moore Aura) last 7-10 years vs 3-5 for budget paint — lower lifetime cost
Get quotes early in the season before contractors fill their books
Ask if there are local utility rebates for low-VOC or reflective coatings (rare but exist in some CA / Pacific NW utilities)
