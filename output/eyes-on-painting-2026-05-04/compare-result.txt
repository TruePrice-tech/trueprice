How to compare painting quotes correctly

Painting quotes are deceptive because most homeowners focus on the price and ignore the prep. A $4,000 exterior quote and a $9,000 exterior quote often differ almost entirely in surface prep — scraping, sanding, priming, and caulking.

Paint brand and line matters more than most realize. Sherwin-Williams Emerald and Benjamin Moore Aura cost 3x the contractor-grade lines and last 2-3x as long. A quote that just says 'premium paint' without a line name is hiding a cheap product.

Always confirm how many coats are included, whether trim, doors, and ceilings are in scope, and what warranty is offered on peeling or fading.
