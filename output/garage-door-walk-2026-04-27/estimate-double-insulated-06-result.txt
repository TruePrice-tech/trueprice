Next Steps

Ways to Save
Manufacturer rebates — Clopay, Wayne Dalton, Amarr, and CHI run seasonal promotions ($50-$200 off)
Bundle door + opener install for crew-day discount (often $100-$200 off vs separate visits)
Off-season scheduling: late fall and winter pricing is often 10-15% lower than spring/summer peak
Insulated doors (R-12 or higher) may qualify for utility rebates in some states — check dsireusa.org
Smart openers (myQ, LiftMaster WiFi) may qualify for home automation/security discounts via your insurance
Ask about cash discount — many garage door companies offer 3-5% off for check or cash payment
Safety Reminders
Garage door springs are under extreme tension. Never attempt DIY spring replacement.
Ensure your installer includes safety sensors and auto-reverse per federal requirements
Ask about wind load rating if you live in a hurricane or high-wind zone
