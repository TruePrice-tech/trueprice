How to compare siding quotes correctly

Siding quotes swing on material, tear-off vs overlay, and what's underneath. Vinyl, fiber cement (Hardie), wood, and engineered wood are all legitimate choices with very different prices and lifespans.

Tear-off is always more expensive than overlay but is the only way to inspect sheathing for rot and install proper house wrap. An overlay quote on a home with moisture issues is a future disaster.

Compare brand, line, insulation backing, trim work, and manufacturer plus workmanship warranty. Hardie installs in particular have strict requirements — a non-certified installer voids the warranty.
