How to compare kitchen remodel quotes correctly

Kitchen remodel quotes are almost impossible to compare at the top line because scope varies enormously. Cabinets alone can swing $15,000+ between builder-grade stock and semi-custom plywood box construction.

Cabinet box construction (particleboard vs plywood), countertop material (laminate vs quartz vs stone), and appliance package drive the biggest cost differences. Plumbing and electrical rough-in work is also commonly excluded from low bids.

Compare demolition and disposal, permit inclusion, and warranty on cabinets and installation separately. A 1-year install warranty is minimum; better contractors offer 2-5 years.

Cabinet brand & line
Cabinet box construction (plywood vs particleboard)
