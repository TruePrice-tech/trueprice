Market Context

This quote is lower than expected. While it could be a good deal, extremely low quotes from movers can sometimes indicate hidden fees, bait-and-switch pricing, or unlicensed operators. Verify the company is licensed and ask for a binding estimate.

The detected hourly rate of $140/hr is market rate for a 3-person crew.

What to Ask Your Mover
Is this a binding estimate or can the price change on moving day?
What valuation/insurance coverage is included? What does full-value protection cost?
Are there extra charges for stairs, long carries, elevators, or bulky items?
What happens if the move takes longer than estimated?
What is the delivery window? Is there a guaranteed delivery date option?
Will my belongings be on a dedicated truck or combined with other shipments?
Verify the company's USDOT number at safer.fmcsa.dot.gov
Ask for a written binding estimate before agreeing to the move
Check online reviews on Google, Yelp, and the BBB
Ways to Save on Your Move
Move mid-week (Tue-Thu) or mid-month for lower rates
Declutter before the move to reduce weight and time
Do your own packing to save on packing labor and materials
Book 4-6 weeks in advance for better availability and pricing
Ask if the mover offers a discount for flexible delivery dates
