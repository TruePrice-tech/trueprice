How to compare plumbing quotes correctly

Plumbing quotes vary because the scope often isn't fully diagnosed until walls are opened. One plumber may quote a spot repair while another quotes a full repipe — both valid, but very different prices. Pipe material alone (PEX vs copper vs CPVC) can swing a quote by thousands.

Watch for fixture brand and model callouts. A quote that says 'new water heater' without specifying gallons, type (tank vs tankless), or brand is hiding $1,000+ in variance. Same with shut-off valves, expansion tanks, and whether old pipe is being removed or left in place.

Compare permit inclusion, warranty length, and cleanup. Licensed plumbers should pull permits for water heater and repipe work — a quote that skips this is cutting a corner you'll pay for at resale.
