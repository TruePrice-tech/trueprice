Oxnard hvac: answers to common questions

Do I need a permit for HVAC replacement in Oxnard?

Yes. Most Oxnard jurisdictions require a mechanical permit for HVAC replacement. Your contractor should pull the permit and schedule the inspection.

When should I replace HVAC in Oxnard?

Off-peak seasons offer the best pricing and availability in Oxnard. Spring and fall are typically 10-20% cheaper than emergency summer or winter replacements.

What SEER rating should I choose?

16 SEER is the sweet spot for most homeowners. Higher ratings save more on energy but have longer payback periods. In hot climates, 18+ SEER pays back faster.

What size HVAC system do I need?

System size depends on home square footage, insulation quality, window area, and climate zone. A Manual J load calculation determines the correct size. Never accept rule-of-thumb sizing.
