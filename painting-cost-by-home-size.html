House Painting Cost by Home Size (2026)

Interior painting in 2026: $1,800-$3,500 for 1000 sq ft, $2,800-$5,500 for 1500 sq ft, $4,500-$9,000 for 2500+ sq ft (walls only). Exterior painting: $2,500-$4,500 for 1000 sq ft, $3,200-$6,500 for 1500 sq ft, $7,500-$13,000 for 2500-3000 sq ft. Prep work, ceiling/trim inclusion, and number of stories move the price.

Painting Cost for a 1500 Sq Ft House

Most-searched specific size. Interior walls-only on a 1500 sq ft single-story: $2,800-$5,500 with mid-grade paint and light prep. Add ceilings: $1,200-$2,000. Add trim/doors: $800-$1,500. Full interior repaint with everything: $4,800-$9,000.

Exterior on the same home: $3,200-$6,500 with standard pressure-wash and light prep. Heavy peeling, primer-required surfaces, or full surface scraping adds $800-$2,500. Two-story version: $4,500-$9,500.

What Moves the Price Beyond Square Footage

Prep work. "Standard prep" means caulk gaps, light sanding, patch nail holes. "Extensive prep" means scraping loose paint, priming bare wood, repairing drywall damage, removing old wallpaper, sanding glossy trim. The prep gap is often $1,500-$5,000 between the same job with different prep needs. Walk through with the painter and identify specifically what prep is included before signing.

Number of coats. Two coats is standard. One coat is sometimes proposed for color-on-color repaints (same color, same sheen) — fine if you trust the painter and the existing surface is sound. Three coats are required for major color changes (white going dark, or vice versa) or for porous surfaces. Three coats ≈ 30-50% more than two.

Paint grade. Builder-grade paint: $25-$35/gallon. Mid-grade (Sherwin-Williams Cashmere, Benjamin Moore Regal): $40-$65/gallon. Premium (BM Aura, SW Emerald, BM Advance): $70-$100+/gallon. The painter's labor cost doesn't change with paint grade, but the materials line item moves $300-$1,000 on a typical job. Premium paint is worth it on high-traffic surfaces (kitchens, bathrooms, hallways) and trim.

Ceiling height. Standard 8-9 ft ceilings: baseline pricing. 10+ ft ceilings: +10-25% for the extra wall area and ladder time. Cathedral or vaulted ceilings: priced individually, often a flat add-on of $400-$1,500 per cathedral area.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to paint a 1500 sq ft house interior?

$2,800-$5,500 for walls only, $4,000-$7,500 with ceilings and trim. Wall area is typically 4,000-5,500 sq ft. Mid-grade paint, standard light prep.

How much does exterior painting cost for a 1500 sq ft house?

$3,200-$6,500 for a single-story 1500 sq ft home with standard prep. Two-story homes of the same floor area cost 15-30% more due to lift access. Heavy peeling or primer-required surfaces add $800-$2,500.

How is painting priced — by sq ft or by room?

Most painters quote per total wall sq ft. Interior wall paint runs $1.50-$3.50 per sq ft of wall in 2026. Per-room flat rates are common ($250-$650 for a standard bedroom).

Is the cost different for a one-story vs two-story home of the same square footage?

Interior: marginal. Exterior: significant — two-story is 20-35% more due to lifts and scaffolding.

What does interior painting cost per square foot in 2026?

$1.50-$3.50 per sq ft of wall area for two-coat work with mid-grade paint. Per floor sq ft equivalent: $2-$6.
