Philadelphia HVAC permits and licensing

Permit authority. Philadelphia Department of Licenses and Inspections (L&I).

Philadelphia's eCLIPSE online portal issues mechanical permits to contractors holding both a PA HICPA registration and a City of Philadelphia contractor license; most residential mechanical permits clear in 3-5 business days and a post-installation L&I inspection is required before PGW will reconnect gas service

PGW requires a released L&I mechanical permit and its own separate gas-company inspection before restoring service after any appliance replacement; the PGW inspector must physically seal the meter before leaving

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new HVAC system cost in Philadelphia?

Should I replace my AC and furnace at the same time?

Replacing both at once is usually more cost-effective than separate replacements. Matched systems run more efficiently and most contractors offer bundle pricing.

What SEER rating should I choose?

14 SEER is the minimum federal requirement. 16 SEER offers a good balance of efficiency and cost. 18-20+ SEER provides the best energy savings but costs significantly more upfront.
