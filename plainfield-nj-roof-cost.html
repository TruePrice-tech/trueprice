Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new roof cost in Plainfield?

Most homeowners in Plainfield pay between $5,900 and $116,750 for a complete roof replacement depending on roof size and materials.

Why do roofing quotes vary so much?

Roofing estimates vary depending on labor assumptions, material brands, flashing work, ventilation upgrades, and warranty coverage included in the proposal.

Should I always choose the lowest roofing quote?

Not always. Lower bids sometimes exclude items like flashing, ventilation upgrades, or disposal costs that appear later as change orders.

My home in Plainfield is older. Does that affect the cost?

Often yes. Homes in Plainfield average around 49 years old. Older roofs may need additional decking repair, updated ventilation, or code-required upgrades that add to the base replacement cost.
