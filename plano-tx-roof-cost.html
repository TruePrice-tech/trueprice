Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new roof cost in Plano?

Most homeowners in Plano pay between $5,500 and $54,350 for a complete roof replacement depending on roof size and materials.

Why do roofing quotes vary so much?

Roofing estimates vary depending on labor assumptions, material brands, flashing work, ventilation upgrades, and warranty coverage included in the proposal.

Should I always choose the lowest roofing quote?

Not always. Lower bids sometimes exclude items like flashing, ventilation upgrades, or disposal costs that appear later as change orders.

Does hail damage affect roofing costs in Plano?

Yes. Plano is in a high hail-risk area. After major storms, contractor demand spikes and wait times can stretch to weeks. Consider impact-resistant (Class 4) shingles, which may also qualify for insurance discounts.
