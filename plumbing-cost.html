Plumbing Cost Guide (2026)

By Geoff Lane, founder of Woogoro · Updated April 2026

Compare water heater replacement, whole house repipe, sewer line, and drain cleaning costs across 700+ U.S. cities.

Plumbing work costs $450 to $8,500 depending on scope. Minor repairs run $150 to $450, water heater replacement averages $1,200 to $3,500, and a whole-home repipe costs $4,500 to $9,500 for PEX or $8,000 to $18,000 for copper. Plumber hourly rates average $85 to $185 in 2026.

What Should a Plumbing Quote Include?

  • Permits and inspections
  • Shut-off valves
  • Cleanup and debris removal
  • Drywall repair / patching
  • Pressure testing
  • Warranty (parts + labor)
  • Old equipment disposal
  • Code compliance upgrades
  • Expansion tank (water heaters)
  • New supply lines
  • Drain pan
  • Venting / flue work
