Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

What TruePrice Covers

TruePrice analyzes contractor quotes (16 home services), auto repair invoices, medical bills and EOBs, legal fee agreements, and moving company estimates. This privacy policy covers all verticals.

How Your Documents Are Processed

When you upload a document (quote, bill, invoice, or agreement), it is processed in two stages:

  • Client-side processing: Text extraction from PDFs and basic parsing happens in your browser. The original file is never uploaded to our servers.
  • AI analysis: Extracted text (not the original file) is sent to our secure API, which uses Anthropic's Claude AI to analyze pricing, detect errors, and generate verdicts. This data is processed in transit and is not stored by TruePrice or Anthropic after analysis is complete.

For image uploads (photos of quotes or bills), the image is sent to our API for AI-powered text extraction and analysis, then discarded.

What Data We Extract

Depending on the document type, TruePrice may extract: pricing data, line items, scope items, CPT codes (medical), hourly rates (legal/moving), labor rates (auto repair), crew size and weight estimates (moving), contractor/provider names, service dates, and location information. This data is used solely to generate your analysis and is not retained after your session.

Medical and Legal Documents

Medical bills and legal fee agreements may contain sensitive personal information. TruePrice treats these documents with the same privacy protections as all other uploads. We do not store, share, or retain the contents of your medical bills, EOBs, retainer agreements, or legal invoices. Analysis results are generated in real-time and not saved on our servers.

Important: TruePrice is not a HIPAA-covered entity. If you are concerned about transmitting protected health information, you may use the manual entry features instead of uploading documents.

Address and Geocoding

When you enter a property address, it is sent to Mapbox for geocoding purposes (converting an address into geographic coordinates). This allows us to provide location-specific pricing data. Mapbox's use of this data is governed by their own privacy policy.

Anonymized Pricing Data

After each analysis, TruePrice automatically stores anonymized, aggregate pricing data to improve our benchmarks and pricing models. This includes: total cost, service type, region (city and state), procedure codes (CPT codes for medical), labor rates, fee structures, and scope item counts. This data does not include: names, addresses, phone numbers, document content, contractor or provider names, dates of birth, insurance policy numbers, or any other personally identifiable information. This data cannot be traced back to any individual user or uploaded document.

Contractor Signup Data

Contractors who sign up for the TruePrice directory provide business information (name, contact, services, license, insurance) which is stored securely. This data is used to verify qualifications and display listings. Contractor data is not shared with third parties.

Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to maintain session tracking information, such as a session ID and analysis history. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any server. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

Cookies

TruePrice does not use cookies or third-party tracking scripts. All analytics are self-hosted.

Third-Party Services

TruePrice uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Document analysis. Data is processed in transit and not retained.
  • Upstash (Redis): Rate limiting and contractor application storage. No document content is stored.
  • Vercel: Hosting and serverless functions.
  • Mapbox: Address geocoding.
  • TruePrice Analytics: Self-hosted aggregate traffic data. No third-party tracking.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We do not sell leads to contractors, repair shops, healthcare providers, or attorneys. Ever.

Data Retention

Uploaded documents: not retained. Analysis results: not stored on our servers. Anonymized pricing data: retained indefinitely to improve benchmarks. Contractor applications: retained until the contractor requests deletion. Analytics data: retained in aggregate form. Email signups: retained until unsubscribe.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any data associated with your email address by contacting us. Since we do not require accounts and do not store uploaded documents, there is typically no personal data to delete beyond email signup records and contractor applications.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@truepricehq.com.
