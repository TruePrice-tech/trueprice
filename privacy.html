Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

What Data We Collect

TruePrice is designed to protect your privacy. When you upload a roofing quote, the file is processed entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your uploaded quotes are not sent to or stored on our servers.

Address and Geocoding

When you enter a property address, it is sent to Mapbox for geocoding purposes (converting an address into geographic coordinates). This allows us to provide location-specific pricing data. Mapbox's use of this data is governed by their own privacy policy.

Community Quote Data

If you choose to submit your quote data to help improve our pricing models, anonymized pricing information (such as total cost, roof size, material type, and region) may be collected. No personally identifiable information such as your name, full address, or contractor details is stored in this process.

Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to maintain session tracking information, such as a session ID and analysis history. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any server. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

Cookies

TruePrice does not use cookies.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@truepricehq.com.
