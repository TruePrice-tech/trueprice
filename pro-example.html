Warranty Interpretation PRO

Always confirm: labor and materials covered separately, what voids it, transferability, who backs it.

Brand & Tier Deep Dive PRO

Asphalt shingle tiers: 3-tab (cheapest, 20-year), architectural (most common, 30-year), premium designer (40+ year). The price difference between architectural and premium is often less than $1,500 on an average roof for a meaningful step up.

Brands worth paying for: GAF Timberline HDZ, CertainTeed Landmark, Owens Corning Duration, Malarkey Vista. All have manufacturer-certified contractor programs that unlock extended warranties.

Underlayment: synthetic underlayment is the modern standard; felt is cheaper and shorter-lived. If your quote shows felt and the price is not significantly lower, push back.
