What Redmond residents ask about painting

How much does interior painting cost per room?

A typical room costs $300-$700 for walls and ceiling. A full interior (5-7 rooms) runs $2,500-$6,000. Trim, doors, and closets add $100-$300 per room.

Is spray painting better than brush and roller?

Spraying is faster and gives a smoother finish on large, flat surfaces. Brush and roller are better for detail work and touchups. Many pros spray first, then back-roll for adhesion.

How often should a house be painted?

Wood siding needs repainting every 5-7 years. Stucco and fiber cement last 8-12 years between coats. Vinyl and aluminum siding rarely need painting. Climate, sun exposure, and paint quality affect timing.

What should I look for in a painting quote?

A professional quote specifies: paint brand and sheen, number of coats, prep work included, surface repairs, primer usage, timeline, warranty, and what is excluded (trim, doors, ceilings).

What shapes exterior painting pricing in Redmond, WA

Why pricing varies in Redmond. Labor rates here run roughly 42% above the national average per BLS metro data, which is the single biggest driver of how this city's pricing differs from the national midpoint. For exterior painting specifically, our cost-multiplier model puts Redmond at roughly 20% above the national midpoint.

Local conditions. Redmond has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Constant moisture in Redmond makes mildew the dominant paint failure mode. Specify 100% acrylic with mildewcide; oil-based paints fail within 3-4 years. Salt air in coastal areas adds corrosion on metal trim and flashing that requires marine-grade primers. Redmond homes averaging 36 years old typically have 1-3 paint layers in good condition. Prep is simpler — power wash, spot-prime bare spots, and apply 2 coats of finish. This keeps the overall project cost lower than older homes with heavy prep requirements.

Permits, codes, and licensing. Most exterior painting projects in Redmond require a permit; confirm with the local building department before signing.

Contractor market. At 57,530 residents, the local pool is moderate; expect 4-6 active contractors for most standard residential work. Redmond's rapid recent growth has tightened the contractor market — expect longer lead times and less negotiating room than smaller markets.
