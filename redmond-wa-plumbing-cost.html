❄

Freeze risk

Redmond occasionally sees freezing temperatures that can threaten exposed pipes. Basic insulation on outdoor spigots and crawlspace pipes is a smart, inexpensive precaution.

🔧

Material recommendation

PEX piping is the most popular repipe material in Redmond. It's flexible, quiet, and typically costs 30-40% less than copper. Copper remains a good choice if you prefer the durability and resale appeal.

💡

Local tip

Homes built in Redmond during the 1970s-80s often have copper pipes that may be developing pinhole leaks after 40+ years. If you're seeing green staining at joints, it's time for an inspection.
