Frequently Asked Questions

How much does attic insulation cost in Rochester?

Most homeowners in Rochester pay between $1,450 and $6,350 for attic insulation, depending on type and coverage area.

Is spray foam insulation worth the extra cost?

Spray foam provides the highest R-value per inch and also acts as an air barrier. Closed-cell foam is best for moisture-prone areas, while blown-in is often the most cost-effective for attic coverage.

How much insulation do I need?

The DOE recommends R-38 to R-60 for attics in most climate zones. An energy audit can determine the exact R-value target for your home in Rochester, NY.
