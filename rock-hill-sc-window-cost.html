Common window replacement questions in Rock Hill

Should I replace window screens too?

New windows almost always include new screens. If reusing inserts, check screen mesh for holes and frame corners for separation. Replacement screens cost $25-$75 each.

Do I need a permit for window replacement in Rock Hill?

In Rock Hill, permits are typically required when changing window size or adding new openings. Same-size replacements usually do not need permits, but check your local building department.

What U-factor should I look for?

Lower U-factor means better insulation. Aim for 0.25-0.30 in cold climates and 0.30-0.40 in mild climates. Energy Star requirements vary by region.

What is Low-E glass?

Low-E (low emissivity) coating reflects infrared heat while allowing visible light through. It reduces heat gain in summer and heat loss in winter, cutting energy costs 10-25%.

Are bay and bow windows more expensive?

Bay windows cost $1,500-$4,000 installed and bow windows $2,500-$5,500. Both require structural support and add 3-4x the cost of a standard same-sized window.
