Rockville fencing: answers to common questions

How deep should fence posts be?

Standard rule: bury one-third of the total post length. For a 6-foot fence with 8-foot posts, dig 24-30 inches deep. In frost-prone areas, posts must go below the frost line to prevent heaving.

How much does vinyl fencing cost?

Vinyl privacy fencing costs $20-$40 per linear foot installed. A typical 150 linear foot backyard fence runs $3,000-$6,000. Vinyl never needs painting, resists rot, and lasts 25-30 years.

Can I install a fence on a slope?

Yes, using either stepped (stair-step) or racked (following the slope) installation. Racked looks smoother on gentle slopes. Stepped is better for steep grades and is structurally stronger.

Who owns the fence between two properties?

Typically the homeowner who installed it. If the fence sits exactly on the property line, both neighbors share responsibility. Always get a survey before building to confirm the boundary.

How long does a wood fence last in Rockville?

In Rockville's climate, a properly installed and maintained pressure-treated fence lasts 15-20 years. Cedar lasts 15-25 years. Untreated pine may only last 5-8 years. Staining or sealing every 2-3 years and keeping vegetation trimmed away from the base are the two most effective ways to extend your fence's lifespan.

Rockville permits: required to build a fence?

Fence permits are required in most Rockville jurisdictions, particularly for heights over 6 feet. Before breaking ground: call 811 for utility locates, verify property lines (a survey costs $300-$600 if boundaries are unclear), and check HOA requirements. A professional contractor handles the permit process as part of the project.

How much does fence installation cost per foot in Rockville?

Per-foot costs for fence installation in Rockville: wood privacy $15-$30, vinyl $20-$40, chain link $10-$20, and aluminum or wrought iron $25-$55. A standard 150-foot backyard fence runs $2,250-$6,000 total. Always compare 3 quotes with the same scope and materials to ensure you are getting fair pricing.
