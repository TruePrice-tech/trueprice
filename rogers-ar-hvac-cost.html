HVAC in Rogers: what locals should know

🌡

Climate and your HVAC

Rogers homeowners typically spend 60-70% of their HVAC energy on cooling. A properly sized, high-efficiency system can cut those costs by 30-40% compared to an aging unit.

⚙

System recommendation

In Rogers, a two-stage or variable-speed AC system runs longer at lower capacity, removing more humidity and using less energy than a single-stage unit cycling on and off.

📅

Best time to buy

The best time to replace your HVAC in Rogers is October through February. Contractors offer off-season pricing and can schedule sooner. Avoid June-September when demand spikes.

💡

Local tip

Rogers contractors are busiest June through September. Book early spring installations to avoid the rush and potential summer breakdowns when you need AC most.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new HVAC system cost in Rogers?

Most homeowners in Rogers pay between $6,035 and $9,603 for HVAC replacement, depending on system type and efficiency.

Should I replace my AC and furnace at the same time?

Replacing both at once is usually more cost-effective than separate replacements. Matched systems run more efficiently and most contractors offer bundle pricing.

What SEER rating should I choose?

14 SEER is the minimum federal requirement. 16 SEER offers a good balance of efficiency and cost. 18-20+ SEER provides the best energy savings but costs significantly more upfront.
