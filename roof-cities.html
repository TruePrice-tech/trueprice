All cities with roof replacement cost data

791 U.S. cities across 50 states · data updated 2026

Roof replacement pricing is dominated by three multipliers that don't show up on a glance — pitch, tear-off depth, and storm-code mandate. A 12:12 cut roof in Asheville costs roughly 60% more in labor per square than a 4:12 ranch in Phoenix even with the same architectural shingle. Hurricane-zone counties in Florida tack on Miami-Dade approval, ring-shank fasteners, and a secondary water barrier; Tornado Alley adds Class-4 impact-rated shingles that materially shift the per-square base. Tear-off cost swings on layer count and the condition of the OSB underneath. The directory below covers every U.S. city where Woogoro has 2026 roof replacement pricing data, including any neighborhood-of-metro pages where local pitch or HOA constraints drive a different number than the parent city.
