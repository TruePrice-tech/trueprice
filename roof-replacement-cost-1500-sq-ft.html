Roof Replacement Cost for a 1500 sq ft House

The average roof replacement cost for a 1500 square foot house typically ranges from $9,000 to $15,000 depending on roofing material, labor costs, and roof complexity.

Typical Roofing Costs for 1500 sq ft Homes

  • Architectural shingles: $9,000 – $13,500
  • Metal roofing: $16,000 – $28,000
  • Standing seam metal: $25,000 – $40,000

Cost Per Square Foot

Most roofing projects for a 1500 sq ft home fall between $4.50 and $7.50 per square foot for asphalt shingles.

Factors That Affect Roof Replacement Cost

  • Roof pitch and complexity
  • Number of penetrations and skylights
  • Material selection
  • Labor costs in your region
  • Permit and disposal costs
