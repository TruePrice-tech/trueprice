Roof Replacement Cost for a 1500 Sq Ft House

The average roof replacement cost for a 1500 square foot house typically ranges from $7,000 to $15,000 depending on roofing material, labor rates, roof complexity, and geographic location.

Most asphalt shingle roof replacements fall between $4.50 and $7.50 per square foot. Premium materials like metal or architectural shingles may increase the total cost.

Typical Roof Replacement Cost for 1500 Sq Ft Homes

  • Basic asphalt shingles: $7,000 – $11,000
  • Architectural shingles: $8,000 – $13,000
  • Metal roofing: $13,000 – $20,000

Cost Per Square Foot

Roof replacement costs are usually calculated per square foot of roof surface. For a 1500 sq ft home, the actual roof surface area is typically 1,600 to 1,900 sq ft depending on pitch and roof design.

This means most homeowners pay:

  • $4.50 – $7.50 per square foot for asphalt roofing
  • $8 – $14 per square foot for metal roofing

How Roof Pitch Affects the Cost of a 1500 Sq Ft Roof

Roof pitch can significantly affect total roofing cost. A steeper roof usually has more actual surface area and requires slower installation and additional safety precautions. Because of this, two homes with the same square footage can have very different roofing prices. Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to replace a 1500 sq ft roof?

Most homeowners pay between $7,000 and $15,000 depending on materials, roof pitch, and local labor costs.

How many roofing squares is a 1500 sq ft roof?

A typical 1500 sq ft home usually requires about 16–19 roofing squares depending on roof slope and design.
