Roof Replacement Cost by House Size

Many homeowners search by house size because they know their interior square footage but not their exact roof size. House size is a useful starting point, though actual roofing cost depends on roof pitch, overhangs, layout, and complexity.

Typical Roof Replacement Cost by House Size

House Size Estimated Roof Size Typical Cost Range
1,500 sq ft home 1,800 to 2,000 sq ft roof $9,000 to $15,000
2,000 sq ft home 2,300 to 2,600 sq ft roof $12,000 to $19,000
2,500 sq ft home 2,800 to 3,200 sq ft roof $15,000 to $24,000
3,000 sq ft home 3,400 to 3,900 sq ft roof $18,000 to $30,000

Why Roof Size Is Bigger Than House Size

A home's roof area is often larger than its interior square footage. Roof pitch, overhangs, garage sections, porches, dormers, and layout complexity all increase the actual roofing area.

That is why two homes with similar interior square footage can still have meaningfully different roofing quotes.

What Affects Roof Replacement Cost

  • Architectural shingles vs metal roofing
  • Roof pitch and complexity
  • Number of valleys, skylights, and penetrations
  • Tear off and disposal requirements
  • Underlayment and flashing scope
  • Ventilation upgrades
  • Local labor and permit costs
