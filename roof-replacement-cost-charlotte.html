Charlotte Roof Replacement Cost (2026)

The average roof replacement cost in Charlotte is often around $12,000 to $22,000 for many homeowners, depending on roof size, material, complexity, tear off requirements, and contractor scope.

Charlotte roofing quotes can vary significantly from one contractor to another. Storm activity, labor demand, disposal costs, and the exact materials included in the estimate all influence where a quote lands.

Before signing, it helps to compare both the total price and the scope details. A lower quote is not always a better quote if it leaves out ventilation upgrades, flashing replacement, underlayment quality, or disposal details.
