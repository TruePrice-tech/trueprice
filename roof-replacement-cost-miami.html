Miami Roof Replacement Cost (2026)

Miami roof replacement costs are often higher than many inland markets because of labor rates, code expectations, weather exposure, and stronger material and installation requirements.

Many Miami homeowners see roof replacement quotes in the rough range of $13,000 to $28,000 or more depending on roof size, roofing system, pitch, tear off, and the exact scope included.

Because coastal conditions and storm resilience matter so much in South Florida, it is especially important to compare roofing proposals by scope, not just price.

What affects roof cost in Miami

  • Roof size and complexity
  • Material type
  • Wind and weather related installation requirements
  • Tear off and disposal
  • Flashing and waterproofing details
  • Contractor workload and local demand
