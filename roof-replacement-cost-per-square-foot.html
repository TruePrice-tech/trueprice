Roof Replacement Cost Per Square Foot

Homeowners often search for roof replacement cost per square foot, but roofers usually price jobs by the roofing square. A roofing square equals 100 square feet of roof area.

What Changes Roof Cost Per Square Foot

Material choice

Material type is one of the biggest cost drivers in any roof replacement estimate.

Roof pitch

Steeper roofs generally cost more because they are more labor intensive and can require added safety measures.

Number of stories

Two story and three story homes usually cost more than single story homes.

Roof complexity

Valleys, dormers, penetrations, and complex layouts can push price higher.

Tear off

A double layer tear off costs more than a single layer removal or an overlay job.

Region

Roofing prices vary by city, labor market, and disposal costs.

Why Roofing Squares Matter

Many homeowners think in square feet, but roofers often think in squares. If your roof is 2,400 square feet, that is about 24 roofing squares. A quote priced at $650 per square would imply a total around $15,600 before adjustments for complexity and scope.
