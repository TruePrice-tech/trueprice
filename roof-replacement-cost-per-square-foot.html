What Affects Cost Per Square Foot

  • Roof pitch and complexity
  • Material type
  • Tear-off and disposal
  • Decking repairs
  • Labor rates by market
  • Contractor margin

Why This Matters

A contractor may quote the same roof at dramatically different prices. Comparing cost per square foot helps normalize quotes and makes pricing easier to understand.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average roof replacement cost per square foot?
Usually around $4 to $11 depending on the material and market.

What is the cheapest roofing option?
Asphalt shingles are generally the lowest-cost mainstream option.

Why are some quotes much higher?
Steep roofs, premium materials, decking repairs, and contractor markup can all raise the quote.
