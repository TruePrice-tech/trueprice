Roof Replacement Cost Per Square Foot

Roof replacement cost per square foot is one of the fastest ways to benchmark a roofing quote. While final pricing depends on materials, complexity, and local labor, cost per square foot gives homeowners a useful starting point.

Average Roof Replacement Cost Per Square Foot

Roof Type Typical Cost Per Sq Ft
Architectural shingles $4 to $8
Metal roofing $8 to $16
Standing seam metal $10 to $18

What Affects Roofing Cost Per Square Foot

  • Material type
  • Roof pitch and complexity
  • Tear off and disposal
  • Flashing replacement
  • Ventilation upgrades
  • Local labor market

Why Roofing Squares Matter

Many homeowners think in square feet, but roofers often think in squares. If your roof is 2,400 square feet, that is about 24 roofing squares. A quote priced at $650 per square would imply a total around $15,600 before adjustments for complexity and scope.
