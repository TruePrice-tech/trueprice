Roof Replacement Cost in Phoenix NC

The average roof replacement cost in Phoenix NC typically ranges between $7,500 and $18,000 depending on roof size, materials, and labor costs.

Most homeowners replacing a standard architectural shingle roof in Phoenix pay around $10,500 to $14,500.

Roof Replacement Cost by House Size

  • 1,500 sq ft house: $7,000 – $10,000
  • 2,000 sq ft house: $9,000 – $13,500
  • 2,500 sq ft house: $11,000 – $16,000
  • 3,000 sq ft house: $13,500 – $19,000

Roof Replacement Cost by Material

  • Architectural shingles: $5 – $7 per sq ft
  • Metal roofing: $9 – $14 per sq ft
  • Standing seam metal: $14 – $22 per sq ft

Factors Affecting Roof Replacement Cost in Phoenix

  • Roof pitch and complexity
  • Number of roof penetrations
  • Material selection
  • Labor demand in the Phoenix market
  • Permit requirements
