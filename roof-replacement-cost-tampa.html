Tampa Roof Replacement Cost (2026)

The average roof replacement cost in Tampa often falls between about $12,000 and $22,000 for many homeowners, depending on roof size, materials, tear off requirements, and contractor scope.

Tampa roofing prices can move meaningfully based on storm activity, labor demand, material selection, and how complete the estimate is. Some quotes include underlayment upgrades, flashing replacement, and disposal, while others leave those items vague.

Typical Tampa roof price range

  • Small homes: $9,000 to $14,000
  • Medium homes: $12,000 to $18,000
  • Large homes: $15,000 to $24,000

What affects roof cost

  • Roof size
  • Shingle type or metal roofing choice
  • Roof pitch and complexity
  • Labor costs
  • Underlayment replacement
  • Contractor markup
