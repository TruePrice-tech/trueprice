Roof Repair vs Roof Replacement

Homeowners often face the same difficult decision: should you repair your roof or replace it entirely? The right answer depends on the roof's age, the severity of damage, and the long term cost of ongoing repairs.

When Roof Repair Makes Sense

  • Roof is less than 10 to 12 years old
  • Damage is isolated to a small area
  • No structural deck damage
  • Leaks are minor and easy to locate

Typical roof repair costs range between $300 and $2,000 depending on the issue.

When Roof Replacement Is Better

  • Roof is over 20 years old
  • Multiple leaks are occurring
  • Shingles are curling or missing
  • Storm damage is widespread

Roof replacement costs often range between $10,000 and $25,000 depending on roof size and materials.

Repair vs Replacement Cost Comparison

Scenario Typical Cost
Minor repair $300 to $1,000
Major repair $1,000 to $3,000
Full replacement $10,000 to $25,000
