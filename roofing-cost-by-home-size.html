Roof Cost by Home Size (2026)

Roof replacement in 2026: $7,500-$10,000 for a 1000 sq ft single-story, $9,500-$13,500 for 1500 sq ft, $13,500-$19,000 for 2000 sq ft, $18,000-$26,000 for 2500-3000 sq ft. Asphalt shingle pricing; metal and tile run 1.5-3x higher. Pitch, complexity, and tear-off depth move the number significantly.

Roof Cost by Home Square Footage

The first thing to understand: roof area is almost never the same as home floor area. A single-story home with a 6/12 pitch and 2-foot overhangs typically has a roof 1.2-1.4x the floor area. A two-story home of the same total square footage has a smaller roof. So tables that price by "home size" are approximations — the contractor's actual measurement is what matters.

The numbers below assume single-story, asphalt architectural shingles (the most common 2026 choice), moderate pitch (4/12-6/12), standard tear-off of one existing layer, and basic flashing replacement. Adjust up for steep pitch, multiple gables/dormers, premium materials, or two-layer tear-off.

Roof Cost for a 1500 Sq Ft House

A 1500 sq ft single-story home in 2026 typically has a roof of about 1,700-2,000 sq ft (17-20 squares). At standard architectural shingle pricing of $475-$700/square installed, the materials-and-labor portion is $8,000-$14,000. Add tear-off ($1,500-$3,000 for a single layer), permit and disposal ($300-$700), and you arrive at the typical full quote of $9,500-$13,500.

Watch for: quotes that don't specify number of squares, quotes that don't explicitly include tear-off (it's separate sometimes), and quotes that omit ice-and-water shield in cold climates. All three are common quote-padding patterns.

Roof Cost for a 2000-2500 Sq Ft House

2000-2500 sq ft single-story usually means 24-33 squares of roof. Architectural shingle: $13,000-$22,500 typical. Premium architectural with upgraded warranty: $16,000-$26,000. Standing-seam metal: $25,000-$42,000. The labor share of any of these is roughly 35-45% of the total — if a quote breaks out labor at 60%+, that's high markup.

Roof Cost for a 3000+ Sq Ft House

3000+ sq ft homes often have complex rooflines (multiple peaks, dormers, valleys), which add significantly to material waste and labor. Expect $22,000-$40,000 for asphalt and $35,000-$65,000+ for metal or tile. Get at least three quotes for any roof of this size — variance between contractors is much larger on big jobs because there's more room for scope ambiguity.

What Moves the Price Beyond Square Footage

Pitch. A 6/12 pitch is the standard reference. 8/12 adds 5-10% labor. 10/12+ adds 15-30% labor and may require harness/scaffolding. Flat roofs (under 2/12) are an entirely different system — TPO/EPDM, not shingles — and aren't directly comparable.

Tear-off layers. Single-layer tear-off: standard pricing. Two-layer tear-off: +$1,500-$4,000. Three-layer (where allowed by code): +$3,000-$7,000. If your existing roof has 2+ layers, code in most states requires a complete tear-off before installing new — no overlay.

Material choice. 3-tab shingle: bottom of range, 20-year life. Architectural shingle: most common, 25-30 year life. Premium / impact-rated: 30-50 year life. Metal: 40-70 year life, 1.5-2x asphalt cost. Tile: 50+ year life, 2-3x asphalt cost. Lifetime cost-per-year often favors metal or impact-rated, but the upfront premium is real.

Decking. If sheathing under the shingles is rotted or damaged (common after storm damage or old leaks), expect $80-$150 per sheet of replacement OSB/plywood. Most quotes include a "decking allowance" that covers a few sheets; widespread rot is billed extra. Ask for a per-sheet rate up front.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a roof cost for a 1500 sq ft house?

$9,500-$13,500 for asphalt architectural shingle on a single-story 1500 sq ft home in 2026. Roof area is typically 1,700-2,000 sq ft. Steep pitches and complex roof lines push the price 20-40% higher.

How is roof size different from home size?

Roof area is almost always larger than the floor area below it. Single-story: 1.05-1.4x floor area depending on pitch and overhangs. Two-story: roof area is roughly half the total home sq ft. Always have the contractor measure rather than assume.

What does a roof cost per square in 2026?

A 'square' is 100 sq ft of roof area. Asphalt 3-tab: $350-$500/square installed. Architectural shingle: $475-$700/square. Premium / impact-rated: $700-$1,000/square. Metal: $850-$1,400/square. Tile: $1,000-$1,800/square.

Is the cost different for a one-story vs two-story home of the same square footage?

Two-story homes of the same total floor area usually cost 10-20% less to re-roof — smaller roof area, but higher labor share due to access. The labor portion is much higher than single-story.

What size roof does a 2500 sq ft house have?

2500 sq ft single-story with 6/12 pitch and standard overhangs: roughly 2,900-3,300 sq ft of roof (29-33 squares). 2500 sq ft two-story: roughly 1,500-1,800 sq ft (15-18 squares). Confirm the actual square count on every quote.
