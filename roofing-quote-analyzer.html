How to use TruePrice

Upload a roofing quote PDF, screenshot, or photo. TruePrice extracts the main pricing and scope details, then estimates whether the quote appears fair for your location, roof size, material, and complexity.

The goal is not just to judge the total price. It is also to help you spot missing items like flashing, drip edge, underlayment, ventilation, or vague scope language that can make one quote look cheaper than it really is.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the quote analysis?

TruePrice estimates whether a roofing quote appears fair based on roof size, material, location, and scope signals found in the estimate. It is designed as a second opinion tool, not a replacement for a contractor inspection.

Can I compare multiple roofing quotes?

Yes. After analyzing your first quote, you can add additional contractor quotes and compare total price, scope signals, and warranty terms side by side.

What file types should I upload?

PDF is ideal, but screenshots, scanned quotes, and phone photos also work. The analyzer uses OCR to extract pricing and scope information from many image based quotes.

Is this tool free?

Yes. The TruePrice analyzer is free to use and does not require an account.

Why can roofing quotes vary so much?

Quotes often differ because of missing scope items, vague line items, lower grade materials, or incomplete flashing and ventilation details. The analyzer highlights signals that may explain large price differences.
