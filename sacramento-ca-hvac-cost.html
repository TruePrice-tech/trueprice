HVAC in Sacramento: what locals should know

🌡

Climate and your HVAC

Sacramento sees some of the highest cooling demands in the country. A two-stage or variable-speed AC handles the extreme afternoon heat more efficiently than single-stage units.

⚙

System recommendation

For Sacramento homes, consider pairing a central AC with a whole-house fan for shoulder-season cooling. When the dry evening air drops to comfortable temperatures, you can cut AC costs significantly.

📅

Best time to buy

Plan ahead in Sacramento: get your AC inspected in March before temperatures climb. If replacement is needed, you'll have time to get multiple bids before the summer rush.

💡

Local tip

In Sacramento, proper attic insulation is as important as the HVAC unit itself. Many homes here have inadequate insulation, forcing the AC to work much harder than necessary.
