What paint type and prep approach works best in Salem?

Constant moisture in Salem makes mildew the dominant paint failure mode. Specify 100% acrylic with mildewcide; oil-based paints fail within 3-4 years. Salt air in coastal areas adds corrosion on metal trim and flashing that requires marine-grade primers.

What red flags should I watch for hiring a painter in Salem?

Any Salem contractor who asks for more than 30% upfront before materials are ordered is a red flag. Standard practice is 10-15% deposit, materials-on-delivery payment, and final payment on completion. Watch for exterior painting quotes in Salem that lack line-item detail. A professional estimate breaks out labor, materials, permits, and cleanup separately. Lump-sum bids hide margin and make change orders impossible to evaluate.
