What fence material works best in Salinas?

Constant moisture in Salinas rots untreated wood fencing within 5-8 years. Cedar is the local favorite for its natural rot resistance, but it still needs sealing every 2-3 years. Vinyl and composite alternatives are gaining market share for their zero-maintenance appeal.

What red flags should I watch for hiring a fence contractor in Salinas?

Watch for fence installation quotes in Salinas that lack line-item detail. A professional estimate breaks out labor, materials, permits, and cleanup separately. Lump-sum bids hide margin and make change orders impossible to evaluate. Check that any Salinas contractor doing fence installation carries both general liability insurance ($1M minimum) and workers' compensation. Request certificates directly from the insurer, not just copies the contractor provides.
