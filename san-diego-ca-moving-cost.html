San Diego and moving companies and licensing

San Diego's moving market benefits from the military community, which creates constant relocation demand. Licensed movers include nationals, SoCal specialists, and military-experienced operations. The California PUC regulates all intrastate movers. The military's permanent change of station (PCS) cycle creates predictable summer demand.

California PUC Cal-T permit required. Interstate movers need FMCSA registration. The military's Transportation Management Office (TMO) at bases provides referral lists for PCS moves.
