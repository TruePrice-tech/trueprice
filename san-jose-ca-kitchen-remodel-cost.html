Induction cooktop conversion from gas requires a new 50-amp 240V circuit ($400-$800) and may trigger panel upgrade if the existing 100-150 amp panel cannot support the additional load. San Jose Plumbing, Water, and Rough-In

Gas tankless (Rinnai, Navien) at $3,000-$4,500 remain popular for their continuous supply. The TECH Clean California program provides $1,000+ incentives for gas-to-electric water heater conversion when bundled with kitchen electrification (induction cooktop), creating a package that makes economic sense to coordinate rather than address separately.
