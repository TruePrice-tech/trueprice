Frequently asked gutter questions

What causes gutters to pull away from the house?

Common causes: rotted fascia board behind the gutter, failed or spaced-too-far-apart hangers, ice and snow weight, clogged gutters holding heavy water, and improper original installation.

Do gutters protect the foundation?

Yes. Gutters prevent concentrated roof runoff from eroding soil next to the foundation and saturating the ground. Homes without gutters have 3-5x more foundation moisture problems than homes with properly functioning gutter systems.

Are gutter guards worth it?

Gutter guards reduce cleaning frequency by 80-90% but do not eliminate it entirely. Quality guards cost $7-$15 per linear foot installed. They pay back in 5-8 years through reduced cleaning costs and prevented water damage.

How many downspouts do I need?

One downspout per 20-30 linear feet of gutter or per 600 square feet of roof area, whichever results in more. Undersized drainage is the number-one cause of gutter overflow and foundation damage.

About gutter installation in Savannah, GA

Where Savannah's pricing comes from. Savannah's labor market runs about 9% below the national median for skilled trades. This wage differential is the primary driver of the area's below-average project costs. On gutter installation, Savannah comes in about 11% under the national average based on our metro pricing data.

Local conditions in Savannah. Savannah has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Gutters in Savannah must handle heavy summer downpours and constant debris from vigorous plant growth. Oversize to 6-inch K-style minimum and add kickout flashing where gutters meet siding — undersized 5-inch gutters overflow during the intense rain events common here. Leaf guards are essential with the year-round vegetation. Gutter guard systems in Savannah range from $5-8 per linear foot for mesh screens to $15-25 for micro-mesh or reverse-curve systems. The payoff depends on tree cover — homes with heavy canopy cover recoup the investment in 3-5 years through eliminated cleaning costs ($150-300 per cleaning, 2-4 times per year).

The Savannah contractor pool. With a population of 142,772, the local market has enough contractors to drive real competition on price, but you should still get three quotes. The Savannah market has grown at a steady pace, producing a solid base of experienced contractors without the severe backlogs of booming cities.
