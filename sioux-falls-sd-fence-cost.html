Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a fence cost in Sioux Falls?

Most homeowners in Sioux Falls pay between $3,750 and $10,800 for fence installation, depending on material and yard size.

What is the cheapest fence material?

Chain link is the most affordable fencing option at $15-$25 per linear foot installed. Wood privacy fencing is the next most affordable at $25-$50 per linear foot.

How long does a wood fence last?

A well-maintained wood fence typically lasts 15-20 years. Regular staining and sealing every 2-3 years can significantly extend its lifespan. Cedar and redwood last longer than pine.

About fence installation in Sioux Falls, SD

Why pricing varies in Sioux Falls. Labor rates here track within a few points of the national average, so most of the price variation comes from material costs and local market dynamics rather than wage differentials. For fence installation specifically, our cost-multiplier model puts Sioux Falls at roughly 8% below the national midpoint.

Local conditions. Sioux Falls experiences intense hailstorms from Plains supercells, with some of the highest per-capita hail damage rates in the nation Sioux Falls sees high hail risk, moderate snow load — all of which contractors here have to plan for. Cedar, vinyl, and metal all work; set posts below the local frost line (36-48 inches) to prevent heaving.

Permits, codes, and licensing. Most fence installation projects in Sioux Falls require a permit; confirm with the local building department before signing.

Contractor market. With a population of 164,676, the local market has enough contractors to drive real competition on price, but you should still get three quotes. Sioux Falls's rapid recent growth has tightened the contractor market — expect longer lead times and less negotiating room than smaller markets.
