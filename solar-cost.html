Solar Panel Installation Cost Guide (2026)

By Geoff Lane, founder of Woogoro · Updated April 2026

Compare solar installation costs by system size, panel type, and city across 1,000+ U.S. locations. Includes 30% federal tax credit calculations.

Residential solar costs $12,500 to $30,000 before incentives for a 10 kW system. After the 30% federal tax credit, net cost runs $8,750 to $21,000. Per-watt pricing averages $2.50 to $3.50 for rooftop and $2.90 to $4.20 for ground-mount arrays. 2026 prices.

What Should a Solar Quote Include?

  • Design and engineering
  • Permits and inspections
  • Solar panels and racking/mounting
  • Inverter (string or microinverters)
  • Electrical wiring and conduit
  • Monitoring system
  • Utility interconnection
  • Roof assessment and any reinforcement
  • Panel warranty (25 yr), inverter warranty (10-25 yr), workmanship warranty
  • Battery storage (optional)
