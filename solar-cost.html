What Should a Solar Quote Include?

  • Design and engineering
  • Permits and inspections
  • Solar panels and racking/mounting
  • Inverter (string or microinverters)
  • Electrical wiring and conduit
  • Monitoring system
  • Utility interconnection
  • Roof assessment and any reinforcement
  • Panel warranty (25 yr), inverter warranty (10-25 yr), workmanship warranty
  • Battery storage (optional)
