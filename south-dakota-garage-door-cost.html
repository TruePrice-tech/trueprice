South Dakota climate, wind & HOA drivers

  • ASCE 7-22 design wind speed: 110 mph (Risk Category II)
  • Hurricane wind tier: No hurricane wind-design exposure
  • Required garage-door R-value: R-13 (IECC 2021 + state amendments)
  • Dominant residential door material: Steel-insulated panel — dominant in most regions, polyurethane or polystyrene foam core
  • Dominant residential opener type: Belt-drive — dominant in attached-garage residential, quietest operation
  • Termite pressure (TPCT zone): Low — minimal termite pressure
  • HOA architectural-control prevalence: Low — limited HOA garage-door restrictions outside metro subdivisions
  • Salt-air corrosion zone: None — no salt-air corrosion exposure
  • IECC climate zone: 5A-6A

South Dakota licensing & permits

  • License status: No statewide trade license
  • License board: South Dakota has no statewide residential builder license — SD Department of Labor & Regulation oversees specialty trades only (official site)
  • Permit: city or county building permit required for new garage-door opening; no state license; replacement-only installs typically permit-exempt; tornado-shelter rough-in within attached garage common in eastern SD municipalities
