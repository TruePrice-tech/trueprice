Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to paint a house exterior in Spokane Valley?

Most homeowners in Spokane Valley pay between $5,650 and $11,450 for exterior painting, depending on home size and paint quality.

How many coats of paint does an exterior need?

Most exterior painting jobs require at least two coats for proper coverage and durability. A single coat may work for touch-ups or if the existing paint is in good condition with a similar color.

How long does exterior paint last?

Quality exterior paint typically lasts 5-10 years depending on climate, sun exposure, and paint quality. Premium paints like Sherwin-Williams Duration can last 10-15 years.
