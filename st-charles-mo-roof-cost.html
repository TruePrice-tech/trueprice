Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new roof cost in St. Charles?

Most homeowners in St. Charles pay between $6,650 and $65,800 for a complete roof replacement depending on roof size and materials.

Why do roofing quotes vary so much?

Roofing estimates vary depending on labor assumptions, material brands, flashing work, ventilation upgrades, and warranty coverage included in the proposal.

Should I always choose the lowest roofing quote?

Not always. Lower bids sometimes exclude items like flashing, ventilation upgrades, or disposal costs that appear later as change orders.

My home in St. Charles is older. Does that affect the cost?

Often yes. Homes in St. Charles average around 41 years old. Older roofs may need additional decking repair, updated ventilation, or code-required upgrades that add to the base replacement cost.
