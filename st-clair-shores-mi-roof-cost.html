Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new roof cost in St. Clair Shores?

Most homeowners in St. Clair Shores pay between $6,650 and $65,800 for a complete roof replacement depending on roof size and materials.

Why do roofing quotes vary so much?

Roofing estimates vary depending on labor assumptions, material brands, flashing work, ventilation upgrades, and warranty coverage included in the proposal.

Should I always choose the lowest roofing quote?

Not always. Lower bids sometimes exclude items like flashing, ventilation upgrades, or disposal costs that appear later as change orders.

Do I need to worry about snow load on my roof in St. Clair Shores?

St. Clair Shores gets significant snowfall, and roofs must be designed to handle the weight. Ice dams are also a concern. Proper ventilation, ice and water shield along eaves, and adequate insulation help prevent costly damage.

My home in St. Clair Shores is older. Does that affect the cost?

Often yes. Homes in St. Clair Shores average around 51 years old. Older roofs may need additional decking repair, updated ventilation, or code-required upgrades that add to the base replacement cost.
