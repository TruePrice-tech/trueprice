Questions St Louis homeowners ask about gutter

How much do new gutters cost in St Louis?

Gutters in St Louis cost $6-$15 per linear foot for aluminum seamless, $8-$20 for steel, and $15-$30 for copper. A typical home (150-200 linear feet) runs $1,200-$3,000 for aluminum.

What causes gutters to pull away from the house?

Common causes: rotted fascia board behind the gutter, failed or spaced-too-far-apart hangers, ice and snow weight, clogged gutters holding heavy water, and improper original installation.

Do gutters protect the foundation?

Yes. Gutters prevent concentrated roof runoff from eroding soil next to the foundation and saturating the ground. Homes without gutters have 3-5x more foundation moisture problems than homes with properly functioning gutter systems.

What St. Louis's local context means for pricing. St. Louis has local climate conditions that affect project scheduling and material choices. Standard 5-inch K-style gutters handle most rain events in St. Louis. Add gutter guards ($5-12 per linear foot) where overhanging trees drop heavy seasonal debris. Leaf debris in gutters promotes standing water and mosquito breeding — seasonal cleaning or guards are important for both function and health. Seamless aluminum gutters are the St. Louis standard: $6-12 per linear foot installed. Copper ($20-40/ft) and zinc ($15-25/ft) offer 50+ year lifespans but at 3-4x the cost.
