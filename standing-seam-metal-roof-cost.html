Standing Seam Metal Roof Cost

Standing seam metal roofs are one of the most durable residential roofing systems available. They are known for long lifespan, weather resistance, and a clean appearance, but they cost significantly more than asphalt shingles.

Average Standing Seam Metal Roof Cost

Roof Size Typical Cost Range
1,500 sq ft roof $15,000 to $27,000
2,000 sq ft roof $20,000 to $36,000
2,500 sq ft roof $25,000 to $45,000
3,000 sq ft roof $30,000 to $55,000

Cost Per Square Foot

Standing seam metal roofing typically costs between $10 and $18 per square foot installed. Exact pricing depends on panel thickness, roof complexity, labor conditions, and flashing details.

Roof complexity can significantly increase installation costs because metal panels require more precision around valleys, chimneys, skylights, and transitions.

Why Standing Seam Roofs Cost More

  • Higher material cost than asphalt shingles
  • Specialized installation skills
  • Longer installation time
  • Precision flashing and trim pieces
  • Higher grade underlayment systems

Standing Seam vs Asphalt Shingles

Roof Type Typical Cost Typical Lifespan
Architectural shingles $4 to $8 per sq ft 20 to 30 years
Standing seam metal $10 to $18 per sq ft 40 to 70 years
