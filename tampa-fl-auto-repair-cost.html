Tampa's auto repair labor rates

Independent shops in the Tampa Bay area charge $80-$130/hour, with South Tampa, Hyde Park, and Davis Islands at the upper end and New Port Richey, Plant City, and Ruskin at the lower end. Dealer service departments bill $145-$265/hour. Tampa's overall pricing is 10-15 percent below Miami because of lower commercial rents and a less congested market.

Tampa's independent shop network is spread along Dale Mabry Highway, Florida Avenue, and the Hillsborough Avenue corridor. South Tampa has premium independents serving the luxury-car market. Brandon and Wesley Chapel have suburban chain-and-independent mixes. Dealer service is standard for warranty work. The military community around MacDill AFB supports a price-sensitive segment.
