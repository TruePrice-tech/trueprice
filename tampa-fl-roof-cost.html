Roof Replacement Cost in Tampa, FL

The average roof replacement cost in Tampa typically ranges between $9,800 and $19,800 depending on roof size, material, pitch, tear off, and complexity.

Typical Tampa Roofing Prices

  • Architectural shingles: $500 to $750 per square
  • Metal roofing: $1,000 to $1,600 per square
  • Tile roofing: $1,500 to $3,000 per square

Tampa Roof Replacement Example

A typical 2,200 sq ft home in Tampa may cost:

  • Low estimate: $11,800
  • Fair range: $14,500 – 17,800
  • High estimate: $20,800+

How to Compare Roofing Quotes

Do not compare roofing quotes by price alone. A lower quote may exclude flashing replacement, ventilation upgrades, underlayment quality, tear off, or disposal.

What Affects Roof Replacement Cost in Tampa?

  • Roof size and number of stories
  • Pitch and jobsite complexity
  • Material selection
  • Local labor and permit conditions
  • Whether old roofing must be removed
