Tempe hvac: answers to common questions

Do I need a permit for HVAC replacement in Tempe?

Yes. Most Tempe jurisdictions require a mechanical permit for HVAC replacement. Your contractor should pull the permit and schedule the inspection.

What's the best season to replace HVAC in Tempe?

Off-peak seasons offer the best pricing and availability in Tempe. Spring and fall are typically 10-20% cheaper than emergency summer or winter replacements.

What SEER rating should I choose?

16 SEER is the sweet spot for most homeowners. Higher ratings save more on energy but have longer payback periods. In hot climates, 18+ SEER pays back faster.
