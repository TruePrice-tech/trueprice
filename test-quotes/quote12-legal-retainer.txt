1. SCOPE OF REPRESENTATION
This firm will represent you in the dissolution of your marriage
to Daniel Torres, including equitable distribution of marital
property, child custody and visitation, child support, and
alimony/spousal support negotiations or litigation.

2. FEE STRUCTURE
Hourly Rate: $375.00 per hour (Attorney Morrison)
Associate Rate: $250.00 per hour (if associate assists)
Paralegal Rate: $125.00 per hour
Billing Increment: 0.25 hours (15-minute increments)

3. RETAINER
Initial retainer deposit: $7,500.00
The retainer will be deposited in our trust account and billed
against as services are rendered. You will receive monthly
invoices showing time entries and remaining balance. When the
retainer balance falls below $1,500, you will be required to
replenish to $5,000.

The initial retainer is NON-REFUNDABLE and constitutes a minimum
fee for opening the case file and initial case assessment.

4. ESTIMATED TOTAL COST
Uncontested with agreement: $5,000 - $8,000
Contested with mediation: $10,000 - $20,000
Contested with trial: $20,000 - $50,000+

5. EXPENSES
Filing fees, service of process, court reporter fees, expert
witness fees, and copying costs will be billed separately at
actual cost plus 15% administrative fee.

6. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this agreement at any time with
written notice. Upon termination, any remaining retainer
balance, less the non-refundable minimum, will be returned
within 30 days.

7. COMMUNICATION
Attorney Morrison will respond to communications within
2 business days. Telephone calls and emails are billed at
the applicable hourly rate.
