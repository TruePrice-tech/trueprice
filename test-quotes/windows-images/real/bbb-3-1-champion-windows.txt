First, regarding the master bathroom window and plantation shutters: At the time of the signing of the contract and installation, it was discussed onsite that the existing plantation shutters would require modification to accommodate the crank mechanism of the new casement windows. The contract clearly states that Champion Windows is not responsible for modification, removal, or reinstallation of existing window treatments, including plantation shutters. This language is outlined in the signed agreement. *** ******* acknowledged completion of the installation by signing the Certificate of Completion at the time the project was finalized. The $500 expense referenced was related to modifications to the homeowner’s existing plantation shutters, not to any defect in the windows themselves. As outlined in the contract and reviewed at installation, these modifications are the homeowner’s responsibility. Therefore, reimbursement is not warranted.
