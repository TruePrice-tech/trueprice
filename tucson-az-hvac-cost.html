Questions Tucson homeowners ask about hvac

Should I replace my AC and furnace at the same time?

Replacing both at once is usually more cost-effective. Matched systems run more efficiently, and you save on labor since the contractor is already on-site.

Do I need a permit for HVAC replacement in Tucson?

Yes. Most Tucson jurisdictions require a mechanical permit for HVAC replacement. Your contractor should pull the permit and schedule the inspection.

Best time of year to replace HVAC in Tucson?

Off-peak seasons offer the best pricing and availability in Tucson. Spring and fall are typically 10-20% cheaper than emergency summer or winter replacements.

What SEER rating should I choose?

16 SEER is the sweet spot for most homeowners. Higher ratings save more on energy but have longer payback periods. In hot climates, 18+ SEER pays back faster.
