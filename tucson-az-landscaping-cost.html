Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a paver patio cost in Tucson?

Most homeowners in Tucson pay between $5,650 and $11,200 for a paver patio, depending on size and materials chosen.

How long does a paver patio last?

A properly installed paver patio can last 25-50 years with minimal maintenance. Pavers can be individually replaced if damaged, making them a long-lasting investment.

Do I need a permit for landscaping work?

Permits are typically required for retaining walls over 4 feet tall, major grading changes, and drainage work that affects neighboring properties. Check with your local building department in Tucson.
