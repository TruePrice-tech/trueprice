Urbandale painting FAQ

What paint sheen should I use?

Flat/matte hides imperfections (ceilings, low-traffic walls). Eggshell and satin work for most walls. Semi-gloss is ideal for trim, doors, and kitchens/baths. High-gloss is for accent trim and furniture.

How much does paint prep work cost?

Prep is 50-70% of the total job cost. Scraping, sanding, caulking, priming, and repairs can cost $1,500-$4,000 on an exterior. Skipping prep leads to peeling paint within 1-2 years.

Do I need to prime before painting?

Prime over bare wood, drywall patches, stains, dark colors being covered with light ones, and previously unpainted surfaces. Paint-and-primer combos work for simple color changes on already-painted walls.
