Electrical & Panel Upgrade Cost in Utah (2026)

Panel upgrades and rewires in Utah typically run $2,200–$5,200 for a 200A service panel upgrade, with the 2020 NEC as the adopted enforcement basis. Service entrance design assumes a 200A minimum on new residential construction. Wasatch Front high-altitude winter inversion drives sustained sub-zero ambients across Salt Lake, Davis, and Utah counties — brittling PVC conduit fittings and forcing rigid metallic conduit on exterior service masts above 5,000 feet elevation — and Bonneville Salt Flats and Great Salt Lake brine deposition corrodes outdoor service equipment on western Utah parcels within 7-10 years.

Utah code & environmental drivers

  • Adopted code basis: 2020 NEC — dwelling unit AFCI on most 120V branch circuits, GFCI expanded
  • State amendments: Utah adopts 2020 NEC under Utah Code Title 58 Chapter 55 with Division of Occupational and Professional Licensing enforcement and Master/Journeyman/Residential tier credentialing
  • Service entrance minimum: 200A for new residential construction
  • Lightning flash density: Low — Pacific coastal and high desert minimal flash density
  • Buried-conduit frost depth: 30-inch+ design depth — buried service conduit must clear hard frost line

Utah licensing & permits

  • License status: Statewide license required
  • License board: Utah Department of Commerce — Division of Occupational and Professional Licensing (Electrical) (official site)
  • Permit: state or municipal electrical permit required for panel upgrade and service-line work

How electrical and panel upgrade costs vary in Utah

State-specific code or insurance rule: Utah DOPL issues a Residential Master Electrician credential as a separately tested classification distinct from the full Master Electrician — the Residential Master is restricted to one- and two-family dwellings up to 200A service, allowing a more accessible entry-level pathway than the Unlimited Master exam, with both credentials requiring 8,000 hours of journeyman experience before sitting for examination.
