Common siding questions in Utica

How do I maintain fiber cement siding?

Repaint every 12-15 years, caulk joints and seams every 5-7 years, keep vegetation trimmed 6 inches from the wall, and wash annually with a garden hose. Avoid power washing at high pressure.

Do I need a permit for siding replacement in Utica?

Most Utica jurisdictions require a permit if you are changing siding material or adding insulation. Like-for-like replacement without structural changes may be exempt. Check with your local building department.

What is board-and-batten siding?

Board-and-batten uses wide vertical boards with narrow strips (battens) covering the seams. It is popular in farmhouse and modern styles. Costs $8-$16/sqft installed in fiber cement or engineered wood.
