Virginia Beach-area vehicle inspections and warranty protections

Virginia requires annual safety inspections ($20) and biennial emissions testing in the Hampton Roads region for vehicles registered in Virginia Beach and surrounding cities. The combined cost runs $36-$48. Virginia State Police oversee the inspection program. Safety inspections cover brakes, tires, lights, suspension, and exhaust. Vehicles that fail must be repaired and reinspected within 15 days at no additional inspection fee.

Virginia's Motor Vehicle Warranty Enforcement Act covers new vehicles with persistent defects within 18 months or 18,000 miles. The Virginia Consumer Protection Act prohibits deceptive auto-repair practices. Virginia law requires shops to provide a written estimate on request and to obtain customer authorization before exceeding the estimate. Military service members are additionally protected by the SCRA (Servicemembers Civil Relief Act) for contracts entered before deployment.
