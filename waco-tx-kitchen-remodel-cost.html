Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a kitchen remodel cost in Waco?

How long does a kitchen remodel take?

A minor cosmetic remodel takes 4-6 weeks. A mid-range remodel takes 8-12 weeks. A major gut renovation can take 3-5 months or longer.

What is the best ROI for a kitchen remodel?

Mid-range kitchen remodels typically recoup 60-80% of their cost at resale. Minor cosmetic updates often provide the highest return on investment.
