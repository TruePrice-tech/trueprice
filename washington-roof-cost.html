Roof Replacement Cost in Washington (2026)

Roof replacement in Washington typically runs $9,000–$23,500 for a 2,000 sq ft asphalt re-roof, with asphalt shingle as the dominant residential covering. The Puget Sound region's persistent moss growth and winter rain drive zinc-strip ridge installations and frequent gutter-debris management; Cascade and Olympic mountain communities (Stevens Pass, Snoqualmie) carry 60–90 psf snow loads.

How roof replacement costs vary in Washington

The Puget Sound region's persistent moss growth and winter rain drive zinc-strip ridge installations and frequent gutter-debris management; Cascade and Olympic mountain communities (Stevens Pass, Snoqualmie) carry 60–90 psf snow loads.

State-specific code or insurance rule: Washington Revised Code §18.27 mandates contractor registration with a $12,000 bond (general) or $6,000 (specialty) and proof of liability/workers' compensation; unregistered roofers cannot file mechanic's liens or collect in court.
