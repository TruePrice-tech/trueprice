Frequently asked painting questions

Do I need to prime before painting?

Prime over bare wood, drywall patches, stains, dark colors being covered with light ones, and previously unpainted surfaces. Paint-and-primer combos work for simple color changes on already-painted walls.

Should I hire a painter or do it myself?

DIY saves 60-70% on cost but takes 3-5x longer and usually looks less polished. Hire a pro for exteriors, high ceilings, detailed trim work, or any spray application. DIY works fine for single interior rooms.

How much does it cost to paint kitchen cabinets?

Professional cabinet painting costs $3,000-$7,000 for an average kitchen. The process includes removing doors, degreasing, sanding, priming, spraying 2-3 coats, and reinstalling hardware.

How much does interior painting cost per room?

A typical room costs $300-$700 for walls and ceiling. A full interior (5-7 rooms) runs $2,500-$6,000. Trim, doors, and closets add $100-$300 per room.

Is spray painting better than brush and roller?

Spraying is faster and gives a smoother finish on large, flat surfaces. Brush and roller are better for detail work and touchups. Many pros spray first, then back-roll for adhesion.

Neighborhood rulebook. If you live in one of West Palm Beach's many HOA-governed neighborhoods, get architectural approval before signing a exterior painting contract. The committee's material and color restrictions may narrow your options, so check first and avoid paying for an unapproved choice.

Local market conditions. With steady but not explosive growth, West Palm Beach's contractor market offers reasonable availability. Most shops can schedule work within 1-3 weeks, and shopping for quotes is straightforward. West Palm Beach's large market supports both premium and budget painting contractors. Prices vary 30-50% for the same scope — the difference is usually prep quality, not paint brand. Ask every bidder to detail their prep process in writing.

When to schedule. In West Palm Beach, the best painting conditions are October through March — cooler temperatures and lower humidity allow proper curing. Summer painting is possible but requires early-morning starts and careful attention to direct sun exposure during application.

What drives cost. Labor costs in West Palm Beach track within a few points of the national average for exterior painting, so material selection and project scope are the bigger pricing levers for homeowners.
