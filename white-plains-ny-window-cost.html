White Plains window replacement FAQ

What is the difference between full-frame and insert replacement?

Insert replacement fits a new window into the existing frame, is faster, and costs less. Full-frame replacement removes everything down to the studs, costs 30-50% more, but fixes hidden damage.

Should I replace window screens too?

New windows almost always include new screens. If reusing inserts, check screen mesh for holes and frame corners for separation. Replacement screens cost $25-$75 each.

Do I need a permit for window replacement in White Plains?

In White Plains, permits are typically required when changing window size or adding new openings. Same-size replacements usually do not need permits, but check your local building department.

What U-factor should I look for?

Lower U-factor means better insulation. Aim for 0.25-0.30 in cold climates and 0.30-0.40 in mild climates. Energy Star requirements vary by region.
