Roof Replacement Cost in Wyoming (2026)

Roof replacement in Wyoming typically runs $7,500–$19,500 for a 2,000 sq ft asphalt re-roof, with asphalt shingle as the dominant residential covering. Mountain communities (Jackson, Pinedale, Sheridan) carry ground snow loads of 50–90 psf and sustained ridge-line wind exposure above 110 mph in exposed sites; standing-seam metal is the dominant high-elevation choice and heavier engineering is routine.

How roof replacement costs vary in Wyoming

Mountain communities (Jackson, Pinedale, Sheridan) carry ground snow loads of 50–90 psf and sustained ridge-line wind exposure above 110 mph in exposed sites; standing-seam metal is the dominant high-elevation choice and heavier engineering is routine.

State-specific code or insurance rule: Wyoming has no statewide contractor licensing — consumer protection runs through Wyoming Statute §40-12 (Consumer Protection Act), which provides treble damages for deceptive home-improvement practices including deductible rebating.
